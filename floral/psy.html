Pansy Doily

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" CROCHET COTTON or - The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED CROCHET 1 ball White or 2 balls Sllver Spangle and The Famous "PURITAN" CROCHET COTTON 1 ball each Shaded Purples and Shaded Yellows steel crochet hook No. 7.
CENTER: With Sllver Spangle ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 5, dc in ring, * ch 2, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 2, join in 3rd st of ch.
2nd Round: Sl st into loop, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * 4 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
3rd Round: * Ch 5, skip 1 dc, sl st in next dc, repeat from * all around ending with ch 2, dc in same space as beginning (this brings thread in poSltion for next round).
4th Round: * Ch 5, sl st in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 5, sl st in dc.
5th Round: Sl st into loop, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 1, 4 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join.
6th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, * ch 4, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, repeat from * around, ch 4, join.
7th Round: Sl st into loop, sc in same space, ch 3, sc in same loop, * ch 5, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 1, trc in 1st sc.
8th Round: Ch 3, dc over trc just made, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 4, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc (shell) in next ch 5 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 4, join in 3rd st of ch.
9th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3 (counts as part of 1st shell), 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 5, shell in center of next shell, repeat from * all around, ch 5, join.
10th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 3, dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, join.
11th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 2, skip 2 dc of same shell, 3 dc in next dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around, ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
12th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 2, skip 2 dc of same shell, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
13th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 2, skip 2 dc of same shell, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
14th Round: Sl st into center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 2, skip 2 dc of same shell, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 3 dc in next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join, cut thread.
PANSY: With Shaded Yellows ch 7, join to form a ring, ch 3, 2 dc in ring, * ch 7, 3 dc in ring, repeat from * 3 times, ch 7, join in 3rd st of ch, cut thread.
2nd Row: Attach Shaded Purples in center dc of any 3 dc group, sc in same space, * 16 dc in next loop, sc in center dc of next 3 dc group, repeat from * twice, ch 4, 12 dtrc (3 times over hook) with ch 1 between each dtrc in next loop, ch 1, 2 trc with ch 1 between in same loop, ch 1, 2 dc with ch 1 between in same loop, sc in center dc of next 3 dc group, 2 dc with ch 1 between in next loop, ch 1, 2 trc with ch 1 between in same loop, ch 1, 12 dtrc with ch 1 between each dtrc in same loop, ch 4, join in 1st sc, cut thread. Work 7 more pansies in same manner. Work 8 pansies in same manner working 1st row in Shaded Purples and 2nd row in Shaded Yellows.
EDGE: Attach Silver Spangle in ch 4 loop of 1st large petal of Yellow Pansy, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 14 times, sc in next sc between petals, sc in next ch 1 space of next large petal of same pansy, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 13 times, ch 3, sc in ch 4 loop of same large petal, sc in 1st ch 4 loop of Purple pansy, work ch 3 loops across the 2 large petals same as on previous pansy. Continue working edge and joining pansies in same manner alternating colors until all pansies are joined, cut thread leaving an end.
TO JOIN PANSlES TO CENTER: Attach Sllver Spangle in middle dc of any dc group of center, sc same space, join to center st of 2nd small petal of 1st pansy (to join: drop loop from book, insert in st, pick up loop and pull through), * sc in same space on center, ch 4, join in 4th dc of next small petal of same pansy, ch 3, 2 dc in middle of next shell on center, ch 3, sl st in top of last dc for picot, 2 dc in same space on center, ch 3, join to 13th dc of 1st small petal of next pansy, ch 4, sc in middle dc of next dc group on center, join to center st of next small petal of same pansy, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread. Sew last pansy to 1st pansy at edge.
