Free Crochet Pansy Doily Pattern

MATERIALS: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 1 ball each White, Shaded Yellows, Shaded Lavenders and Kelly Green.
Approximate size: 12 inches in diameter.
Steel crochet hook No. 7.

Center: With White or Silver Spangle chain (ch) 6, join to form a ring, ch 5, double crochet (dc) in ring, * ch 2, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 2, join in 3rd stitch (st) of ch (8 spokes).
2nd Round: Ch 1 and work 3 single crochet (sc) in each loop, join (24 sc) .
3rd Round: Ch, 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 sc, * ch 2, 1 dc in each of the next 3 sc, repeat from * all around, ch 2, join in 3rd st of ch.
4th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, * ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around, ch 2, join.
5th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, * ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around, ch 2, join.
6th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, * dc in next dc, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
7th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, dc in next dc, * ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
8th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, ** dc in next dc, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, * ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * once, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
9th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, ** dc in next dc, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, * ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * twice, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
10th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, ** 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, * ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 3 times, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
11th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, ** 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, * ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * twice, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
12th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, ** 1 dc in each of the next 6 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, * ch 1, dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * once, 1 dc in each of the next 6 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
13th Round: Ch 3, dc in same space, * 1 dc in each of the next 8 dc, dc in next ch 1 space, dc in next dc, dc in next ch 1 space, 1 dc in each of the next 8 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
14th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, * ch 3, skip 2 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ch 3, skip 2 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join, cut thread.
15th Round: Attach Green in ch 2 loop between solid sections, ch 3, 2 dc in same space keeping last loop of each dc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 3, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc (shell) in next loop, ch 4, cluster st in next loop (cluster st: 3 dc in same space keeping last loop of each dc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time) , ch 4, shell in next loop, ch 3, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, ch 3, cluster st in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st, do not cut Green.
Pansy: With Shaded Yellow or Yellow Spangle ch 7, join to form a ring, ch 3, 2 dc in ring, * ch 7, 3 dc in ring, repeat from * 3 times, ch 7, join in 3rd st of ch.
2nd Row: Slip stitch (sl st) to next dc, * 16 dc in next loop, sc in center dc of next dc group, repeat from * twice, ch 4, 12 double treble crochet ([dtrc] 3 times over hook) with ch 1 between each dtrc in next loop, ch 1, 2 treble crochet (trc) with ch 1 between in same loop, ch 1, 2 dc with ch 1 between in same loop, sc in center dc of next dc group, 2 dc with ch 1 between in next loop, ch 1, 2 trc with ch 1 between in same loop, ch 1, 12 dtrc with ch 1 between each dtrc in same loop, ch 4, join in sl st, cut thread. Work 7 more pansies in same manner. Work 8 more pansies with Shaded Lavender or Purple and Silver Spangle in same manner.
To join pansies to center pick up Green, * ch 4, sc in same space for picot, ch 3, join to 13th dc of 1st small petal of Yellow pansy, ch 4, sc in center of next shell on Center, join to center of next small petal of same pansy, sc in center of same shell on Center, ch 4, join to 4th dc of next small petal of same pansy, ch 3, sc in top of next cluster st on Center, repeat from * all around until all pansies are joined and alternating color of pansies while joining, join, cut thread.
Edge: Attach White or Silver Spangle in ch 4 loop of 1st large petal of any pansy, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 14 times, sc in next sc between petals, sc in next ch 1 space of next large petal of same pansy, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 13 times, ch 3, sc in ch 4 loop of same large petal, sc in 1st ch 4 loop of next pansy, work ch 3 loops across the 2 large petals same as on previous pansy. Continue in same manner across the 2 large petals of all pansies, join, cut thread.
