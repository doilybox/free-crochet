The Pansy Doily

MATERIALS:
Approximate Size 12 1/2 inches in diameter This doily can be made with the following: The Famous "PURITAN" CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 or "DE LUXE" QUALITY CROCHET COTTON, Article 346 1 ball each White, Kelly Green and Tinted Lavender & Yellow Steel crochet hook No. 7
PANSY With Tinted Lavender and Yellow ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 3, 2 dc in ring, * ch 7, 3 dc in same space, repeat from * twice, ch 10, 3 dc in same space, ch 10, join in 3rd st of ch. Place a marker at joining.
2nd Round: Sl st in next st, * 9 sc in next loop, skip 1 dc of next dc group, sl st in next dc, repeat from * twice, 11 dc in next loop, sdc in same space (sdc: thread over hook, insert in space, pull loop through, thread over and work off all loops at one time), 2 sc in same space, sl st in center dc of next dc group, 2 sc in next loop, sdc in same space, 11 dc in same space, sl st in joining.
3rd Round: Skip the next sl st and 1 sc, * sc in next sc, 2 sc in each of the next 2 sc, sc in next sc, 2 sc in each of the next 2 sc, sc in next sc, skip 1 sc, 1 sl st and 1st st of next petal, repeat from * twice, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in each of the next 6 dc, sc in next st, sc in next st, skip 1 sc, 1 sl st and 1st st of next petal, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in each of the next 6 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, sdc in next dc, sc in next dc, skip 1 st, join.
4th Round: Skip 1 st, * 1 sc in each of the next 4 sc, 2 sc in next sc, 1 sc in each of the next 4 sc, skip 2 sc, repeat from *twice, sc in next st, sdc in next st, dc in next st, 2 dc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 4 sts, trc in next st, 2 trc in next st, trc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, trc in next st, 2 dc in next st, sdc in next st, skip 4 sts, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next st, trc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, trc in next st, 2 trc in next st, trc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 4 sts, 2 dc in next st, dc in next st, sdc in next st, 1 sc in next st, skip 1 st, join.
5th Round: *Sc in next st, sdc in next st, dc in next st, 2 dc in next st, 1 dc in each of the next 2 sts, 2 dc in next st, dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, repeat from * twice, skip 1 st, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next st, 2 trc in next st, trc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 trc in next st, 2 trc in next st, repeat from * 5 times, trc in next st, 2 dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, skip 2 sts, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next st, trc in next st, * 2 trc in next st, 1 trc in next st, repeat from * 5 times, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 trc in next st, 2 trc in next st, 2 dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, skip 1 st, join.
6th Round * Skip 1 st, sc in next st, sdc in next st, dc in next st, 2 dc in next st, 1 dc in each of the next 2 sts, 2 dc in next st, dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, skip next st, repeat from * twice, skip next st, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next st, 2 trc in next st, trc in next st, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, trc in next st, 2 trc in next st, repeat from * 7 times, trc in next st, 2 dc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 sdc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sts, skip 6 sts, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sts, 1 sdc in each of the next 2 sts, 2 dc in each of the next 2 sts, trc in next st, * 2 trc in next st, 1 trc in next st, repeat from 7 times, 2 trc in each of the next 2 sts, tr c in next st, 2 trc in next st, 2 dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, skip 1 st, join.
7th Round: * Skip 1 st, sc in next st, sdc in next st, 2 dc in next st, dc in next st, 2 dc in each of the next 2 sts, dc in next st, 2 dc in next st, sdc in next st, sc in next st, skip next st, repeat from * twice, sl st in space between skipped sc and next sc.
Next Round: * Ch 3, sl st in next st, repeat from * around entire outer edge, ch 3, join, cut thread.
Sew pansies as illustrated sewing the 8th loop on side of small petals together (there should be 28 free loops on lower edge of each pansy between the joinings).
CENTER OF DOILY: With Kelly Green ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 3, 2 dc in ring, * ch 5, 3 dc in same space, repeat from * 3 times, ch 1, trc in 3rd st of ch (this brings thread in position for next round).
2nd Round: Ch 1, sc over trc, ch 5, sc over same st, * ch 5, 1 sc, ch 5, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 5, join in trc, cut thread.
3rd Round: Attach White in any loop over a loop of preceding round, ch 4, trc in same space, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc (shell) in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, join in 4th st of ch, cut thread.
4th Round: Attach Green in center of any shell, ch 4, trc in same space, ch 3, join to 5th loop of inner edge of any pansy to right of joining (to join: sl loop off hook, insert in loop of pansy, pull loop through), * ch 3, 2 trc in same shell, ch 3, skip 4 loops of same pansy and 1st 4 loops of next pansy, join in next loop, ch 3, 2 trc in same shell, ch 3, skip next 5 loops of same pansy, join in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop of center of doily, ch 3, skip 3 loops of same pansy, join in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop of center of doily, ch 3, skip 2 loops of same petal, join in next loop, ch 3, 2 trc in next shell, ch 3, skip 5 loops of same pansy, join in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch, cut thread.
OUTER EDGE: With right side of work toward you attach White in 5th loop from center of any pansy, sc in same space, * ch 5, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ch 3, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, skip remainder of loops of pansy, sc in 17th loop of next pansy, ch 3, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, * ch 5, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, skip remainder of loops of petal, sc in 5th loop from center of next petal, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
2nd Round: Sl st to center of loop, sc in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ** ch 7, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 14 times, repeat from ** 3 times, ch 7, skip 2 loops, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, join in 1st sc.
3rd Round: Sl st to 2nd st of 1st loop, sc in same loop, ch 3, sc in same space, * ch 9, skip 1 loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ** ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc (shell) in next loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, * ch 9, skip 1 loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * times, repeat from ** 3 times, ch 3, shell in next loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, * ch 9, skip 1 loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 4, tr trc (4 times over hook) in 1st sc.
4th Round: Ch 3, sc over tr trc, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * twice, ** ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * 5 times, repeat from ** 3 times, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, tr trc in tr trc.
5th Round: Ch 3, sc over tr trc, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * twice, ** ch 3, shell in next ch 7 loop, ch 1, shell in next shell, ch 1, shell in next ch 7 loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * 4 times, repeat from ** 3 times, ch 3, shell in next ch 7 loop, ch 1, shell in next shell, ch 1, shell in next ch 7 loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, ch 9, join in tr trc, cut thread.
6th Round: Attach Green in center of 1st shell of any shell group, sc in same space, ch 3, sc in same space, ** ch 4, 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc (dc shell) in next shell, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next shell, ch 4, skip the next ch 3 loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 3 loop, * ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join in 1st sc, cut thread.
7th Round: Attach White in center of dc shell, sc in same space, ch 3, sc in same space, * ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * 12 times, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
8th Round: Attach Green in ch 3 loop above dc shell, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 3 loop, ch 3, dc shell in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch, cut thread.
Next Round: Ch 6, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, * ch 3, dc in next sc, ch 3, sl st in top of dc just made for picot, repeat from * 9 times, ** ch 3, thread over hook, insert in next sc, thread over and work off 2 loops, thread over hook, skip the sc that joins the 6 loops together, insert hook in next sc, thread over and work off 2 loops, thread over and work off remaining loops at one time, ch 3 picot, * ch 3, dc in next sc, ch 3 picot, repeat from * 9 times, repeat from ** all around ending with ch 3, dc in next sc, join in 3rd st of ch under the picot, cut thread. Lightly press.
