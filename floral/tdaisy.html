Three Way Doily

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 2 balls White 1 ball each Shaded Lt. Pinks, Buttercup and Nile Green will make all 3 doilies Approximate size round doily: 12 inches in diameter Approximate size small oval doily: 10 inches x 13 inches Approximate size large oval doily: 10 inches x 16 inches or The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MER-CERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 4 balls Silver Spangle 2 balls each Pink Spangle, Sunshine Spangle and Chartreuse Spangle will make 3 doilies Approximate size round doily: 13 1/2 inches in diameter Approximate size small oval doily: 12 inches x 16 1/2 inches Approximate size large oval doily: 12 inches x 20 inches Steel crochet hook No. 7
ROUND DOILY MOTIF-CENTER
With Buttercup or Sunshine Spangle, chain (ch) 5, join to form a ring, ch 1 and work 8 single crochet (sc) in ring, join in 1st sc.
2nd Round: 2 sc in each sc, join, cut thread. Work another center in same manner.
3rd Round: Place wrong sides of centers together. Attach Green or Chartreuse Spangle in any sc, working through both thicknesses and inserting a little cotton between sections, * ch 3, sc in next sc, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.
4th Round: Attach Pink in any ch 3 loop, ch 4, [counts as 1 treble crochet (trc)], trc in same space, ch 4, slip stitch (sl st) in same space, * 1 sl st, ch 3, 1 sl st in next loop, sl st in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc in same space, ch 4, sl st in same space, repeat from * 6 times, 1 sl st, ch 3, 1 sl st in next loop, sl st in next loop, ch 4, join in 4th St of ch.
5th Round: Drop loop from hook, insert in 4th st of ch just made, pull loop through, ch 4, 1 trc in each of the next 3 stitches (sts) keeping last loop of each st on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 11, skip the next ch 3 loop, 1 trc in each of the next 4 sts keeping last loop of each st on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, repeat from * 6 times, ch 11, join.
6th Round: Sl st to 2nd st of loop, 5 sc, ch 3, 5 sc in same loop, * ch 3, 5 sc, ch 3, 5 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, join, cut thread.
7th Round: Attach White or Silver Spangle in center of any ch 3 loop, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc (shell) in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 3, join in 4th st of ch.
8th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
9th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next space, * ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around, ch 6, join.
10th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond. join.
11th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 9, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around, ch 9, join.
12th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
13th Round: Sl st to center of shell, sc in same space, * ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip 1 loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip 1 loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, double crochet (dc) in 1st sc. (This brings thread in position for next round).
14th Round: ** Ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending with ch 3, dc in dc.
15th Round: ** Ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around, join in dc, cut thread.
16th Round: Attach Buttercup or Sunshine Spangle in the 1st of any of the two-ch 6 loops, ** ch 3, sc in next ch 6 loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, repeat from ** all around, join, cut thread.
17th Round: Attach Green or Chartreuse Spangle in 1st loop before the ch 3 loop, ** ch 5, skip the ch 3 loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from ** all around, join, cut thread.
18th Round: Attach Pink in 1st loop before the ch 5 loop, ** ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, sc in next loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, repeat from ** all around, join.
19th Round: Sl st into loop, 2 sc in same space, ** ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, * ch 3, 2 sc, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
OVAL DOILY Work motif same as for round doily. Work another motif in same manner, joining to 1st motif in 6th round as follows: sl st to 2nd st of loop, 5 sc in same loop, * ch 1, join to corresponding loop of 1st motif, ch 1, 5 sc in same loop of 2nd motif, ch 3, 5 sc in next loop, repeat from * once, finish motif same as 1st motif, cut thread. This completes the center of doily. Working around entire center, attach White or Silver Spangle in ch 3 loop of 3rd scallop of motif at right hand side from joining, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, 2 trc, ch3, 2 trc (shell) in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * 4 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, shell in next loop, * ch 3, shell in next loop, repeat from * 11 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, * shell in next loop, ch 3, repeat from * 6 times, join in 4th st of ch.
2nd Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 3 loops keeping last loop of each trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time (trc joining), * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * 10 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, work a trc joining, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
3rd Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, sc in top of trc joining, ch 4, * shell in next shell, ch 6, repeat from * 9 times, shell in next shell, ch 4, sc in joining, ch 4, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
4th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, * trc in next shell keeping last loop of st on hook, repeat from * once, thread over and work off all loops at one time (trc joining), ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, work a trc joining, ch 7, sc in next loop, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
5th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in center of shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in top of trc joining, ch 6, sc in next loop, * ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in center of next shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, finish round to correspond ending with ch 3, dc in last sl st (this brings thread in position for next round).
6th Round: * Ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, dc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 6, sc in next loop, ** ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, repeat from ** 3 times, finish round to correspond ending with ch 3, dc in dc.
7th Round: * Ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, dc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, ** ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, repeat from ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, join, cut thread.
8th Round: Attach Buttercup or Sunshine Spangle in next loop, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, repeat from ** once, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, join, cut thread.
9th Round: Attach Green or Chartreuse Spangle in last loop worked, ** ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from ** once, ch 5, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ** ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, join, cut thread.
10th Round: Attach Pink in last loop worked, ** ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, sc in next loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, repeat from ** all around, join.
11th Round: * Insert hook in next loop, draw thread through, repeat from * once, thread over and pull through all loops at one time, * ch 2, 2 sc, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ** ch 2, * insert hook in next loop, draw thread through, repeat from * once, thread over and pull through all loops at one time, * ch 2, 2 sc, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, repeat from ** all around, ch 2, join, cut thread.
LARGE OVAL DOILY Work 2 motifs joining same as for small oval doily. Work a 3rd motif joining to 2nd motif in same manner having 2 free points between joinings. This completes the center of the doily. Working around entire center attach White or Silver Spangle in ch 3 loop of 3rd scallop of end motif at right hand side from joining, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc (shell) in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * 4 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, shell in next loop, * ch 3, shell in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, shell in next loop, * ch 3, shell in next loop, repeat from * 11 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, shell in next loop, * ch 3, shell in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in joining, * shell in next loop, ch 3, repeat from * 6 times, join in 4th st of ch.
2nd Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 3 loops keeping last loop of each trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time (trc joining), * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * twice, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 3 loops keeping last loop of each trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * 10 times ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, trc joining in next 3 loops, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * twice, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, trc joining in next 3 loops, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
3rd Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, sc in top of trc joining, ch 4, shell in next shell, * ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * once, ch 4, sc in top of trc joining, ch 4, shell in next shell, * ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * 9 times, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
4th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4, 1 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, * trc in next shell keeping last loop of st on hook, repeat from * once, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, * trc in next shell keeping last loop of st on hook, repeat from * once, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 trc, ch 5, 1 trc in next shell, repeat from * 3 times, finish round to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
5th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in center of next shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, * ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in top of trc joining, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, * ch 7, skip next loop, 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next shell, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, finish round to correspond, ending with ch 3, dc in last sl st (this bring thread in position for next round).
6th Round: * Ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ** ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, dc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from ** once, ** ch 3, trc in next trc, * ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, ending round with ch 3, dc in dc.
7th Round: * Ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, ** ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, dc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from ** once, ** ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * once, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond join, cut thread.
8th Round: Attach Buttercup or Sunshine Spangle in next loop, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, repeat from ** once, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 8 times, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, join, cut thead.
9th Round: Attach Green or Chartreuse Spangle in last loop worked, ** ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from once, ** ch 5, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from last ** once, ** ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, repeat from last ** 3 times, finish round to correspond, join, cut thread.
10th Round: Attach Pink in last loop worked, ** ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, sc in next loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, repeat from ** all around, join.
11th Round: * Insert hook in next loop, pull loop through, repeat from * once, thread over and pull through all loops at one time, * ch 2, 2 sc, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ** ch 2, * insert hook in next loop, pull loop through, repeat from * once, thread over and pull through all loops at one time, * ch 2, 2 sc, ch 3, 2 sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, repeat from ** all around, ch 2, join, cut thread.
