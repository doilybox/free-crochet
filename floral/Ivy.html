Ivy Rose Doily

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 2 balls -White 1 ball Shaded Yellows or
The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 5 balls Silver Spangle 2 balls Pink Spangle.
Steel crochet hook No. 7
Approximate size: 25 inches in diameter Center of Doily is worked after Wreath of Flower Motifs are completed.
Flower Motif: With Shaded Yellows or Pink Spangle, chain (ch) 8, join to form a ring, ch 7, * double crochet (dc) in ring, ch 3, repeat from * 6 times, join in 4th stitch (st) of ch (8 spaces) .
2nd Round: * Single crochet (sc) in next loop, short double crochet (sdc) in same space (sdc: thread over hook, insert in st; pull loop through, thread over and work off all loops at one time) , 1 dc, 1 treble crochet (trc) , 1 dc, 1 sdc, 1 sc in same space (1 petal completed) , repeat from * all around, join with a slip stitch (sl st) in 1st sc.
3rd Round: * Ch 5, sl st between next 2 petals, repeat from * all around, ch 5, join in 1st sl st.
4th Round: * 1 sc, 1 sdc, 1 dc, 1 trc, 1 double treble crochet ([dtrc] 3 times over hook) , 1 trc, 1 dc, 1 sdc, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join in 1st sc, cut thread.
5th Round: Attach White or Silver Spangle in dtrc of any petal, * ch 5, sl st in next dc of same petal, ch 5, sl st in next dc of next petal, ch 5, sl st in next dtrc of same petal, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join (24 loops) .
6th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, sc in same loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join in 1st sc.
7th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, sc in same loop, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join in 1st sc (24 picot loops) , cut thread.
2nd Motif: Work 1st 6 rounds same as 1st motif.
7th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, sc in same loop, ch 3, join in picot of any loop of 1st motif (to join: drop loop from hook, insert in picot, pick up loop and pull through) , * ch 3, sc in next loop of 2nd motif, ch 3, join in next picot of next loop of 1st motif, repeat from * once, ch 3, sc in next loop of 2nd motif, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2, sc in next loop of same motif, repeat from * 20 times ending last repeat with sc picot already worked in, cut thread.
3rd Motif: Sl st to center of next loop, sc in same loop, ch 3, join to 10th picot of 2nd motif, * ch 3, sc in next loop of 3rd motif, ch 3, join in next picot of next loop of 2nd motif, repeat from * once, ch 3, sc in next loop of 3rd motif, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2, sc in next loop, repeat from * 20 times ending last repeat with sc in picot already worked in, cut thread. This leaves 9 picot loops between each flower on either side free. Work 15 more motifs in same manner joining last motif to 1st motif (18 motifs in wreath).
Center of Doily: Work 1st 6 rounds same as flower motif.
7th Round: Repeat 6th round.
8th Round: Sl st into next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 1, 2 dc in same loop, * 2 dc, ch 1, 2 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in, 3rd st of ch.
9th Round: Sl st into ch 1 space, ch 3, dc in same space, * ch 5, 2 dc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 3rd st of ch.
10th Round: Sl st between next 2 dc, * ch 6, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc between next 2 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch.
11th Round: ** Sc in next loop, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, repeat from * once, ch 2, skip next loop, repeat from ** all around, join.
12th Round: Sl st to center of next loop between picots, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, repeat from * once, ch 2, sc in next loop between picots, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in sl st between picots.
13th Round: Sl st to center of next loop between picots, * ch 6, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, repeat from * once, ch 3, sc in center of next loop between picots, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in sl st.
14th Round: Sl st to center of next loop between picots, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 6, 3 dc in center of next loop between picots, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join in 3rd st of ch.
15th Round: Sl st in next dc, ch 6, * dc in next loop, ch 2, dc in same loop, ch 3, dc in center dc of next 3 dc group, ch 3, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch.
16th Round: Sl st into next loop, ch 6, * 3 dc in next ch 2 loop, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, skip next ch 2 loop, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch.
17th Round: Sl st in next loop, ch 3, ** dc in same loop, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 2 dc in next loop, * ch 3, dc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 3, dc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch.
18th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 6 dc, ** 2 dc in next loop, * ch 3, dc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, 2 dc in next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 7 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
19th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 8 dc, * 2 dc in next loop, ch 2, dc in next loop, ch 2, 2 dc in next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 11 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
20th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 10 dc, * 2 dc in next loop, ch 2, 2 dc in next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 15 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
21st Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 11 dc, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next loop, ch 2, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 17 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
22nd Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 10 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * once, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 15 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd St of ch.
23rd Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 9 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * twice, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 13 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
24th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 8 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * 3 times, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 11 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
25th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 7 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * 4 times, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 9 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
26th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 6 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * 5 times, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 7 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
27th Round: Sl st to next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * 6 times, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
28th Round: Sl st to next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ** ch 2, skip next dc, * dc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * 7 times, skip next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
29th Round: Sl st to next dc, ch 6, ** dc in next loop, * ch 3, dc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ch 3, dc in center dc of next 3 dc group, ch 3, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
30th Round: ** Sc in next loop, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, repeat from * once, ch 2, skip next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
31st Round: Sl st to center of next loop between picots, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2, sc in center of next loop between picots, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in sl st at center of 1st loop, do not cut thread.
Joining Join Wreath to center of doily as follows: sl st to center of next loop between picots of doily, ** ch 3, 4 dc cluster st in next 4 picots (4 dc cluster st: keeping last loop of each dc on hook, 1 dc in 8th and 9th picot of any flower, 1 dc in 1st and 2nd picot of next flower, thread over and pull through all loops at one time) , * ch 3, join in next picot of same flower, ch 5, join in center st between picots of next loop of doily, repeat from * 4 times, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join, cut thread. Weave in all ends. Press lightly. Starch if necessary.
