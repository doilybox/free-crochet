Valley Lily - Free Crochet Doily Pattern

MATERIALS: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 2 balls White 1 ball Violet Approximate size 21 inches in diameter or The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 4 balls Silver Spangle 1 ball Blue Spangle or colors of your choice
Approximate size: 22 inches in diameter and "STAR" MERCERIZED EMBROIDERY COTTON, Article 50 1 Skein Medium Orange
Steel crochet hook No. 7.
1st Round: With White or Silver Spangle chain (ch) 6, join to form a ring, ch 6, double crochet (dc) in ring, * ch 3, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, join in 3rd stitch (st) of ch.
(8 ch 3, spaces).
2nd Round: Slip stitch (sl st) into loop, ch 4 (counts as 1 treble crochet (trc) , 1 trc in same space, * ch 1, 3 trc, ch 1, 3 trc in next loop, ch 1, 2 trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 1, 3 trc, ch 1, 3 trc in next loop, ch 1, join in 4th st of ch.
3rd Round: Ch 4, * trc in next trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 3, 1 trc in next trc, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 3, join in 4th st of ch.
4th Round: Ch 4, * trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc in same trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, trc in same trc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.

6th Round: Ch 4, * trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 sts, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, trc in next trc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
7th Round: Ch 4, * trc in next trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
8th Round: Sl st between next 2 trc, ch 7, * trc in next loop, ch 3, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 3, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc (4 trc cluster: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 4 sts, thread over and work off all loops at one time) , ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 3, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 3, trc between next 2 trc, ch 3, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
9th Round: Ch 8, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
10th Round: Ch 8, trc in next loop, ** ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
11th Round: Ch 8, ** trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 4, 4 trc cluster over next group of 4 trc, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 4, 4 trc cluster over next group of sts, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
12th Round: Ch 9, ** trc in next loop, * ch 5, trc in next loop, repeat from * 14 times, ch 5, trc in next trc, ch 5, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
13th Round: Ch 9, ** trc in next loop, * ch 5, trc in next loop, repeat from * 15 times, ch 5, trc in next trc, ch 5, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.

18th Round: Ch 4, ** trc in same space, * 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, repeat from * once, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, skip next ch 2 loop, trc in next loop, * ch 5, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 2, skip next ch 2 loop, trc in next trc, repeat from ** all around, ch 2, join in 4th st of ch.
19th Round: Ch 4, ** trc in same space, * 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 4, 2 trc in next trc, repeat from * once, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, skip next ch 2 loop, trc in next loop, * ch 5, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 2, skip next ch 2 loop, trc in next trc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch I2, join in 4th st of ch.

21st Round: Ch 4 (always counts as part of 1st cluster st), trc in next trc, ** 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, * 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 4 trc, repeat from * once, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 2, skip next loop, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join in top of 1st cluster.
22nd Round: Ch 4, 1 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, * 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc. ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, repeat from * once, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 1, 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, repeat from ** all around, ch 1, join in top of 1st cluster.
23rd Round: Ch 4, keeping last loop of each st on hook, work 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ** ch 7, * sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 3, 2 trc cluster in next trc, ch 7, repeat from * 4 times, sl st in 4th st from hook, ch 3, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc group, ch 7, sl st in 4th st from hook, ch 3, * 2 trc cluster in next trc, ch 7, sl t in 4th st from hook, ch 3, repeat from * 4 times, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc group, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc group, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
Flower: With Lavender or Blue Spangle ch 4, join to form a ring, * ch 3, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 2 dc in ring, thread over and work off all 3 loops at one time, ch 3, sl st in ring, repeat from * until 6 petals are completed, cut thread, fasten securely. Work 23 more flowers in same manner. With Medium Orange, embroider a French Knot in center of each flower. Sew in position as illustrated. Block to measurements.
