Sunburst Doily

MATERIALS:
For doily 20" in diameter: Daisy Mercerized Crochet Cotton, size 20, 2 skeins: steel crochet hook No. 9.
lst Rnd: Starting at center, ch 5. Dc in first ch, (ch 1, dc in same ch) 6 times; ch 1, sl st in 4th ch of ch-5.
2nd Rnd: Ch 3, dc in sl st just made, (ch 1, 2 dc in next dc) 7 times; ch 1, sl st in 3rd ch of ch-3.
3rd Rnd: Ch 3, dc in sl st just made, 2 dc in next dc, * ch 2, (2 dc in next dc) twice. Repeat from * 6 times more: ch 2, sl st in 3rd ch of ch-3.
4th Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next 3 dc, * ch 3, dc in next 4 dc. Repeat from * 6 times more, ch 3. Join as before.
5th Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next 2 dc, * ch 3, dc in first 2 dc of next group, ch 3, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * 6 times more, ch 3. Join.
6th Rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * in next ch-3 make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next dc and ch, dc in next ch, ch 8, skip next dc, dc in dc. Repeat from * around, ending ch 3. Join.
7th Rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * 2 dc in ch-2, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in ch-3, dc in dc, dc in next ch-3, ch 3, skip next dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
8th Rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in ch-3, dc in next dc, in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc, dc in ch-3, ch 3, skip next dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
9th Rnd: Sl st between next 2 sts, ch 6, * dc in sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in sp, ch 3, dc between next 2 dc, ch 3. Repeat from * around, ending with tr in 3rd ch of ch-6.
10th Rnd: Ch 6, * dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp, ch 3. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
11th Rnd: Ch 5, * dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, 10 tr in ch-5 sp, ch 2, skip next sp and next dc, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around, ending 10 tr in ch-5, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc. Join with sl st to 3rd ch of ch-5.
12th Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next sp, dc in next dc, * ch 2, tr in next tr, (ch 1, tr in next tr) 9 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, dc in next sp, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
l3th Rnd: Ch 3, * in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc, dc in next sp, ch 2, sc in next ch-1 sp, (ch 3, sc in next ch-1) 8 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
14th Rnd: Ch 5, * dc in ch-2, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc, dc in sp, ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 7 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in 2 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join with sl st to 3rd ch of ch-5.
15th Rnd: Sl st in next sp, ch 3, * dc in next dc, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in sp, ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 6 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
l6th Rnd: Ch 3, * in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp, ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 5 times; ch 2, dc in sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in sp, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
17th Rnd: Ch 5, * dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp) twice; ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 4 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
18th Rnd: Sl st in next sp, ch 8, * dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp) twice; ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 3 times; (ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
l9th Rnd: Sl st in sp, ch 4, 9 tr in same sp, * (ch 2, skip next sp and next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp) twice; ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) twice; (ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc) twice; ch 2, 10 tr in ch-5. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
20th Rnd: Ch 5, * (tr in next tr, ch 1) 8 times; tr in next tr, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in sp) twice; ch 2, sc over first loop, ch 3, sc over next loop, (ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc) twice: ch 2, (tr in next tr, ch 1) 9 times; tr in next tr. Repeat from * around, ending in established pattern. Join.
2lst Rnd: Sl st in first ch-1 sp, ch 1, sc in same sp, (ch 3, sc in next ch-1) 8 times; * (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp) twice; ch 2, sc over next loop, (ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc) twice: ch 2, (sc in next sp, ch 3) 8 times; sc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join with sl st to first sc.
22nd Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 7 times; ch 2, dc in next sp and dc, in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next 2 sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in sp and next dc, in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc, dc in next dc, dc in next sp, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
23rd Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 6 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc, dc between two 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc in next sp, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
24th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 5 times; (ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
25th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 4 times; (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
26th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 3 times; (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) twice; dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) twice; ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
27th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) twice; (ch 2, dc in next sp and next 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in sp, dc and sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
28th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over first loop, ch 3, sc over next loop, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) 3 times; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
29th Rnd: Sl st over loop, ch 1, * sc over loop, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) 3 times; ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) 3 times; ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
30th Rnd: Sl st in next 2 ch, ch 3, * dc in next 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and next sp) twice; (dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2) twice; dc in next sp, dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and sp) 3 times; dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
31st Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) 3 times; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
32nd Rnd: Ch 5, * (dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2) 3 times; dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) 3 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 3 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
33rd Rnd: Sl st in next 2 ch, ch 3, * dc in next 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, dc and sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) 3 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in dc, ch 2, dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
34th Rnd: Ch 8, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) 3 times; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; dc in next sp and 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc and next sp) 3 times; dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
35th Rnd: * Ch 5, (dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2) 3 times; dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) 3 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
36th Rnd: Sl st in next 2 ch, ch 3, * dc in next 2 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 8 dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in sp, ch 5, dc in next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) 3 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 3 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
37th Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, skip next sp, 10 tr in ch-5, ch 2, skip next sp and dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, 10 tr in ch-5, ch 2, skip next sp and dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
38th Rnd: Ch 5, * (dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2) twice: tr in first tr, (ch 1, tr in next tr) 9 times; (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice: (dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2) twice; tr in next tr, (ch 1, tr in next tr) 9 times; (ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp) 3 times; dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
39th Rnd: Sl st in next 2 ch, ch 3, * dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, sc in first ch-1 sp, (ch 3, sc in next sp) 8 times; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, sc in first ch-1 sp, (ch 3, sc in next sp) 8 times; (ch 2, skip next dc. dc in next 2 dc and next sp) twice; ch 2, skip next dc, dc in 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
40th Rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, ch 2, sc over first ch-3 loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 7 times: ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, ch 2, skip dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, ch 2, sc over next ch-3 loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 7 times; ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in same dc, dc in next dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join and break off.
PINEAPPLE: Attach thread in next ch-2 sp from where it was broken off. Ch 5, * skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, sc over first loop, (ch 3, sc over next loop) 6 times; ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, sc over next ch-3, * ch 3, sc over next ch-3. Repeat from * across to within ch-2, ch 2, dc in ch-2 and in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5. Ch 5, turn. Continue working in this manner, until one ch-8 loop remains. Then work as follows: Skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, dc over ch-3, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, 2 sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in center dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 3, turn; dc in 3rd ch of ch-5. Break off.
SMALL SCALLOP: Attach thread in same sp where last dc of first row of pineapple was made; ch 5, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, 2 sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in 3 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; dc in center dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 3, turn; dc in 3rd ch of ch-5. Break off.
LARGER SCALLOP: Attach thread in same sp where last dc of first row of pineapple was made; ch 5, (skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2) twice; skip next dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc) twice; ch 2, dc in next sp, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, 2 sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and sp, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups and in next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, ch 2, skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc and next sp, ch 2, dc in center dc, ch 2, dc in next sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, 2 sp and 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 2 dc, dc between 3-dc groups and next 2 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; skip next dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 5, turn; dc in center dc, ch 2, dc in 3rd ch of ch-5, ch 3, turn; dc in 3rd ch of ch-5. Break off.
Work one rnd of sc closely around entire edge. Work 2 more rnds of sc having 3 sc in tip of each scallop and skipping first and last 2 sc on each scallop. Join and break off.
