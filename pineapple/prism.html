Pineapple Prism Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:
1 ball of No. 1 White, No. 61 Ecru or No. 42 Cream.
Doily measures 14 inches in diameter
Milwards Steel Crochet Hook No. 7.

Starting at center, ch 2. 1st rnd: Make 8 sc in 2nd ch from hook. Join with sl st to first sc.
2nd rnd: Ch 3, dc in joining - starting cluster made; holding back on hook the last loop of each dc, make 2 dc in next sc, thread over and draw through all loops on hook - cluster made; ch 3, (cluster in each of next 2 sc, ch 3) 3 times. Join to first dc.
3rd rnd: In tip of starting cluster make starting cluster and cluster; 2 clusters in tip of next cluster, ch 5, (2 clusters in tip of each of next 2 clusters, ch 5) 3 times. Join to first dc.
4th rnd: In tip of starting cluster make starting cluster and cluster, * cluster in tip of each of the next 2 clusters, 2 clusters in tip of next cluster, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, 2 clusters in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3, sc in last sp, ch 3. Join to first dc.
5th rnd: Starting cluster in tip of starting cluster-starting cluster over cluster made; * cluster in tip of next cluster - cluster over cluster made; ch 3, sc between next 2 clusters, ch 3, skip next cluster, cluster over next cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 2 and dc; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 2 and cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with dc in tip of first cluster used at beg of rnd, ch 2. Join to first dc.
6th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) twice; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join as before.
7th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 3 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 4, dc in next dc, ch 5, dc in next dc, ch 4. Repeat from * around. Join.
8th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop ch 3) 4 times; (cluster over cluster) twice: (ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc) twice; ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join.
9th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 5 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 4, dc in next dc, ch 5, dc in next dc, ch 4. Repeat from * around. Join.
10th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 6 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; (ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc) twice; ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join.
11th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (se in next loop, ch 3) 7 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 5, dc in next dc, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join.
12th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 8 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 2, dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join.
13th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 9 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 1, dc in next dc, ch 5, dc in next dc, ch 1. Repeat from * around. Join.
14th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 10 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
15th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 11 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; (ch 5, dc in next dc) twice; ch 5. Repeat from * around. Join.
16th rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 10 times; skip next loop, cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; (ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc) twice; ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with dc in tip of first cluster used at beg of rnd, ch 3. Join.
17th rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 9 times; skip next loop, cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 4 times; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
18th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 8 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, sc in next ch-3 sp, ch 3, (dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3) 6 times. Repeat from * around. Join.
19th rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 7 times; cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 6 times; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
20th rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 6 times; cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; ch 3, sc in next ch-3 sp, eh 3, (dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3) 8 times; in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
21st rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 5 times; cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 10 times; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
22nd rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 4 times; (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, sc in next ch-3 sp, ch 3, (dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3) 12 times. Repeat from * around. Join.
23rd rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) 3 times; cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 12 times; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
24th rnd: (Cluster over cluster) twice; * ch 3, skip next loop, (sc in next loop, ch 3) twice; cluster over cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 3 and dc; ch 3, sc in next ch-3 sp, ch 3, (dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3) 14 times; in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and cluster; cluster over cluster. Repeat from * around. End and join as on 16th rnd.
25th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, dc in next dc, (ch 5, dc in next dc) 15 times; ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join.
26th rnd: * (Cluster over cluster) twice; skip next 2 loops, (cluster over cluster) twice; ch 3, sc in next ch-3 sp, ch 3, (dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3) 16 times. Repeat from * around. Join.
27th rnd: Ch 3, making 1 dc in tip of each of first 2 clusters, make a 2-dc cluster over these 2 clusters, * making 1 dc in tip of next cluster and 2 dc in tip of following cluster, make a 3-dc cluster over these 2 clusters; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 16 times; ch 5, making 2 dc in tip of next cluster and 1 dc in tip of following cluster, make a 3-dc cluster over these 2 clusters. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
28th rnd: Ch 3, making 1 dc in tip of first cluster and 2 dc in tip of next cluster, make a 3-dc cluster over these 2 clusters, * (ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc) 16 times; ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, making 2 dc in tip of each of next 2 clusters, make a 4-dc cluster over these 2 clusters. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join.
29th rnd: Ch 6, dc in joining, * (ch 5, dc in next dc) 7 times; (ch 5, in next dc make dc, ch 3 and dc) twice; (ch 5, dc in next dc) 7 times; ch 5, in tip of next cluster make dc, ch 3 and dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join to 3rd ch of ch-6.
30th rnd: Ch 6, sc in first ch-3 sp, ch 3, * dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join to 3rd ch of ch-6. Break off and fasten.
Press lightly through a damp cloth.
