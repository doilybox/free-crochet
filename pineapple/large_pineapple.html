The Large Pineapple Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:

1st Round: Ch 6, join to form a ring, ch 1 and work 8 sc in ring, join in 1st sc.
2nd Round: * Ch 10, sc in next sc, repeat from * 6 times, ch 4, dtrc (3 times over hook) in same space as beginning (this brings thread in position for next round) .
3rd Round: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 7, 3 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, ch 7, join.
4th Round: Sl st in next dc, ch 1, sc in same space, * ch 5, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sc in center dc of next dc group, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
5th Round: Sl st into 2nd loop, ch 3 (counts as part of 1st cluster st), dc in same space, ch 5, 2 dc cluster st in same space (2 dc cluster st: 2 dc in same space keeping last loop of each dc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time), repeat from * twice, * ch 5, skip 2 loops, 4 - 2 dc cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st in next loop, repeat from * all around, ch 5, join.
6th Round: Sl st to center of loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
7th Round: Sl st to center of loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, skip 1 loop, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 6, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
8th Round: Sl st into loop, ch 3, dc in same space (the ch 3 at beginning of each round always counts as part of 1st cluster st), ch 4, 2 dc cluster st in same space, * ch 7, skip 1 loop, 2 - 2 dc cluster sts with ch 4 between in next loop, ch 7, skip 1 loop, 2 - 2 dc cluster sts with ch 4 between in next loop (cluster st shell), repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join each round. All shells are cluster st shells and will be referred to as shells for remainder of doily.
9th Round: Sl st in center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 6, sc in center st of next loop, ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
10th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
11th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, skip 1 loop, 3 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
12th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 6, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 6, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
13th Round Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 9, skip 1 sc, sc in next sc, ch 9, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
14th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
15th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 8, skip 1 loop, 3 dc in next loop, ch 8, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
16th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ch 4, 2 dc cluster st in same space, * ch 8, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 8, skip 1 loop, 3 cluster sts with ch 4 between each cluster st in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
17th Round: Sl st into loop, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, shell in next loop between the cluster sts, ch 9, sc in center sc of next sc group, ch 9, skip 1 loop, shell in next loop between 1st 2 cluster sts, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
18th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
19th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, skip 1 loop, 9 dc in loop, ch 4, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, ch 6, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, ch 6, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
20th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 9 dc with ch 1 between each trc, ch 3, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, skip 2 loops, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
21st Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 4, sc between 1st 2 trc, * ch 4, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 6 times, ch 4, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond.
22nd Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 4, skip 1 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 4, sc in next loop, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond.
23rd Round: Same as last round but having 1 loop less across each pineapple.
24th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 4, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 4, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond.
25th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, work 3 - ch 4 loops across pineapple, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
26th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, work 2 - ch 4 loops across pineapple, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
27th Round: Same as last round but having 1 loop less across each pineapple and 1 - ch 5 loop more between pineapples.
28th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 5, sc in remaining loop of pineapple, ch 5, skip 1 loop, shell in next shell, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond.
29th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next sc, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 2, shell in next loop, ch 2, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
30th Round: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * skip 2 loops, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 2, skip the ch 2 loop, 3 cluster sts with ch 4 between each cluster st in next shell, ch 2, sc in next ch 5 loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
31st Round Sl st into loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ** ch 2, skip 1 loop, shell in next loop between 1st 2 cluster sts, ch 4, shell in next loop, ch 2, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, repeat from ** all around ending with ch 2, dc in sl st.
32nd Round: ** Ch 2, cluster st in next loop, * ch 5, sl st in 3rd St from hook for picot, ch 2, cluster st in same loop, repeat from * twice, ch 2, sc in next loop, ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2, sc in next loop, ch 2, skip 1 loop, cluster st in next loop, ch 5, sl st in 3rd st from hook for picot, ch 2 (picot loop), cluster st in same space, * work a picot loop, 2 cluster sts with picot loop between in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 2, skip the ch 2 loop, sc in next loop, work a picot loop, sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around, join, cut thread. n in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 2, skip the ch 2 loop, sc in next loop, work a picot loop, sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around, join, cut thread.
