3rd Row: Ch 4, trc in next trc joining, **ch 4, trc in same space, 7 trc in center st of corner loop, trc in next trc joining, *ch 4, work a trc joining working 1 trc in same space and 1 trc in next trc joining, repeat from *once, repeat from **all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
4th Row: Ch 4, trc in next trc, *ch 4, 2 trc in each of the next 7 trc, ch 4, work a trc joining working 1 trc in next trc and 1 trc in next trc joining, ch 4, work a trc joining working 1 trc in same space and 1 trc in next trc joining, ch 4, work a trc joining working 1 trc in same space and 1 trc in next trc, repeat from *all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
5th Row: **Ch 4, trc in next trc, *ch 1, trc in next trc, repeat from *12 times, ch 4, sc in next trc joining, ch 4, 5 trc in next trc joining, ch 4, sc in next trc joining, repeat from **all around.

7th Row: Sl st into ch 3 loop, ch 4, 4 trc in same loop, drop loop from hook, insert in 4th st of ch, pick up loop and pull through, *ch 2, popcorn st in next ch 3 loop (popcorn st: 5 trc in same space, drop loop from hook, insert in 1st trc, pick up loop and pull through), repeat from *10 times, ch 7, 5 trc with ch 2 between each trc in trc cluster, ch 7, skip 1 loop, popcorn st in next ch 3 loop, repeat from 1st *all around ending to correspond, ch 7, join.
8th Row: Sl st in 1st ch 2 loop, *ch 3, sc in next ch 2 loop, repeat from *9 times, ch 6, trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, 3 trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 6, skip 1 loop, sc in next ch 2 loop of pineapple, repeat from 1st *all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join in sl st.
9th, Row: Sl st into ch 3 loop, ch 4 (counts as part of 1st popcorn st in each row), popcorn st in same space, *ch 2, popcorn st in next ch 3 loop, repeat from *8 times, ch 6, 2 trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 2 trc in next trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next trc, ch 6, popcorn st in next ch 3 loop, repeat from 1st *all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join.

11th Row: Sl st into ch 3 loop, ch 4, *work a popcorn st in each ch 3 loop with ch 2 between each popcorn st across pineapple, ch 7, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 6, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc, ch 6, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 3, trc in next trc, ch 3, 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, ch 7, skip 1 loop, repeat from *all around ending to correspond, ch 7, join.

19th Row: Sl st into loop, ch 6, trc in same space, *ch 2, trc in same space, repeat from *twice, **ch 5, sc in next loop, *ch 8, sc in next loop, repeat from *3 times, start corner: ch 5, trc in next trc keeping last loop of trc on hook, 1 trc in next trc joining keeping last loop of trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, *ch 4, trc in same space and 1 trc in next trc joining keeping last loop of each trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time, repeat from *10 times (this completes corner), ch 5, sc in next loop, *ch 8, sc in next loop, repeat from *3 times, ch 5, 5 trc with ch 2 between each trc in next ch 3 loop, repeat from **all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 4th st of ch.
