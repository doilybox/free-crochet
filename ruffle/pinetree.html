Pine Tree Doily

MATERIALS:
J. & P. COATS BIG BALL BEST SIX CORD MERCERIZED CROCHET, Art. A.104, Size 30: 2 balls of Ecru; or CLARK'S BIG BALL MERCERIZED CROCHET, Art. 8.34, Size 30: 2 balls of No. 61 Ecru. Milwards Steel Crochet Hook No. 10. Starting at center, ch 8. Join with sl St to form ring.
1st rnd: Ch 5, (dc in ring, ch 2) 7 times. Join to 3rd ch of ch-5 (8 sps).
2nd rnd: Sl st in first sp, ch 3, 2 dc in same sp, * ch 2, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2. Join to top of ch-3.
3rd rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from around, ending with ch 2. Join.
4th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
5th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next 5 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 3, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
6th rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * dc in next 6 dc, ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
7th rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * dc in next 4 dc, ch 4, sc in next sp, sc in next sc, sc in next sp, ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
8th rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, * dc in next 2 dc, ch 5, sc in next sp, sc in next 3 sc, sc in next sp, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
9th rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, holding back on hook the last loop of each dc, make 2 dc in same dc, thread over and draw through all loops on hook (cluster made); * ch 7, sc in next sp, sc in next 5 sc, sc in next sp, ch 7, skip 1 dc, make a 3-dc cluster in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
10th rnd: Sc in same place as sl st, * ch 5, skip 3 sc, in next sc make (tr, ch 2) 3 times and tr; ch 5, sc in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around. Join.
11th rnd: Ch 3, make a 2-dc cluster in same place as sl st, * ch 5, (dc in next tr, ch 1) twice; in next sp make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 1, dc in next tr) twice; ch 5, make a 3-dc cluster in next sc. Repeat from *around. Join.
12th rnd: Sc in same place as sl st, * ch 5, (dc in next dc, ch 1) 3 times; in next sp make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 1, dc in next dc) 3 times; ch 5, sc in next cluster. Repeat from * around. Join.
13th rnd: Ch 3, 2-dc cluster in same place as sl st, * ch 5, (dc in next dc, ch 1) 4 times; in next sp make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 1, dc in next dc) 4 times; ch 5, cluster in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
14th rnd: Sc in same place as sl st, * ch 5, (dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp) 4 times; dc in next dc, in next sp make 2 dc, ch 2 and 2 dc; (dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp) 4 times; dc in next dc, ch 5, sc in next cluster. Repeat from * around. Join.
15th rnd: Sl st to center of next loop, sc in same loop, * ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next 4 dc, (ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 2, in next sp make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc) 3 times; dc in next 3 dc, (ch 5, sc in next loop) twice. Repeat from * around ending with ch 2, dc in first sc.
16th rnd: Ch 3, 2 dc in loop just formed, *ch 5, sc in next sp, ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next dc, (2 dc next sp, dc in next dc) 3 times; in next sp make 2 dc, ch 2 and 2 dc; (dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp) 3 times; dc in next dc, ch 5, sc in next sp, ch 5, 3 dc in next loop. Repeat from * around. Join to top of ch-3.
17th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next sp, sc in next sp, ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next 4 dc, ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, in next sp make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc) twice; dc in next 3 dc, ch 5, sc in next sp, ch 5, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
18th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 6, skip next sp, sc in next sp, ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next dc, (2 dc in next sp, dc in next dc) twice; in next sp make 2 dc, ch 3 and 2 dc; (dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp) twice; dc in next dc, ch 5, sc in next sp, ch 6, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
19th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, * dc in next 5 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 7, skip next sp, sc in next sp, ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next 4 dc, ch 2, in next sp make dc, ch 3 and dc; ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next 4 dc, ch 5, sc in next sp, ch 7, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
20th rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, dc in next 6 dc, * (ch 5, sc in next sp) twice; ch 5, skip 3 dc, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp, dc in next dc, in next sp make 2 dc, ch 3 and 2 dc; dc in next dc, 2 dc in next sp, dc in next dc, (ch 5, sc in next sp) twice; ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next 7 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
21st rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, dc in next 4 dc, * (ch 5, sc in next loop) 3 times; ch 5, skip 3 dc, sc in next dc, ch 5, in next sp make dc, ch 5, (trc, ch 5) twice, and dc, ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 3 times; ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next 5 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
22nd rnd: Sl st in next dc, ch 3, holding back on hook the last loop of each dc, make dc in next 2 dc, thread over and complete a cluster, * (ch 5, sc in next loop) 6 times; ch 5, in next sp make dc, ch 5, (tr, ch 5) twice and dc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 6 times; ch 5, skip 1 dc, make a cluster over next 3 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in first cluster.
23rd rnd: (Ch 5, sc in next loop) 8 times; * ch 5, in next sp make dc, ch 5, (tr, ch 5) twice and dc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 16 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in dc.
24th rnd: * 10 dc in next loop, sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 8 times; ch 5, in next sp make dc, ch 5, (tr, ch 5) twice and dc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 9 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in dc.
25th rnd: * Dc in next 5 dc, ch 3, dc in next 5 dc, sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 9 times; ch 5, in next sp make dc, ch 5, (tr, ch 5) twice and dc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 10 times. Repeat from * around. Join as before.
26th rnd: * Dc in next 5 dc, 5 dc in next sp, dc in next 5 dc, 3 sc in each of next 2 loops, 4 dc in each of next 9 loops, 10 dc in next loop, 4 dc in each of next 9 loops, 3 sc in each of next 2 loops. Repeat from * around, ending with 3 sc, sl st in dc.
RUFFLE:
1st rnd: Sc in same place as sl st, (ch 5, skip 1 st, sc in next st) 3 times; * ch 5, skip 1 st, in next st make (sc, ch 5) 3 times and sc; (ch 5, skip 1 st, sc in next st) 26 times; ch 5, skip 1 st, in next st make (sc, ch 5) 4 times; in next st make (sc, ch 5) 3 times and sc; (ch 5, skip 1 st, sc in next st) 26 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in first sc.
2nd to 5th rnds incl: Ch 5, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in dc. At end of 5th rnd, ch 5, sl st in dc. Break off. Starch lightly and press.
