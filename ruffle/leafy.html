Leafy Spray Doily

MATERIALS:
J. & P. COATS BIG BALL BEST SIX CORD MERCERIZED CROCHET, Art. A.104, Size 30: 2 balls of White; or CLARK'S BIG BALL MERCERIZED CRO-CHET, Art. B.34, Size 30: 2 balls of White.
Milwards Steel Crochet Hook No. 10.
Doily measures 16 inches in diameter
Starting at center, ch 10. Join with sl st to form a ring.
1st rnd: Ch 3, dc in ring, ch 1, (2 dc in ring, ch 1) 9 times. Join with sl st in top of ch-3.
2nd rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 3, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join.
3rd rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 5, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from *. around. Join.
4th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 1, in center ch of next sp make dc, ch 3 and dc; ch 1, dc in each of next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
5th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 3, skip next sp, 3 dc in center ch of next sp, ch 3, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join.
6th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 3, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
7th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 3, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around.. Join.
8th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next 7 dc, ch 4, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4. Join.
9th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next 5 dc, ch 5, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
10th rnd: Sl st in next dc and in next ch, ch 3, dc in next ch, * ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 4, skip next 3 ch, dc in next 2 ch, ch 5, dc in next 2 ch. Repeat from * around. Join.
11th rnd: Sl st in next dc and in next ch, ch 3, dc in next ch, * ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 4, skip 3 ch, dc in next 2 ch, ch 4, dc in center ch of next sp, ch 4, dc in next 2 ch of next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
12th rnd: Sl st in next dc and in next ch, ch 3, dc in next ch, * ch 5, dc in 3rd and 4th ch's of next sp, ch 4, in next dc make dc, ch 3 and dc; ch 4, skip next sp, dc in next 2 ch. Repeat from * around. Join.
13th rnd: Sl st in next dc and in next ch, ch 3, dc in next ch, * ch 1, skip 1 ch, dc in next 2 ch, ch 4, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next sp, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, ch 4, skip next sp, dc in next 2 ch. Repeat from * around. Join.
14th rnd: Sl st in next dc and in next ch, * ch 3, dc in same sp, ch 4, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, in next dc make dc, ch 3 and dc; ch 3, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 4, skip next sp, 2 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. Join.
15th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next sp, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip 1, dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 4, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
16th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next sp, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, (dc in next dc, ch 3) twice; dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next sp, dc in next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
17th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, 2 dc in next dc, * dc in next 2 ch, ch 5, skip 1 dc of next group, dc in next 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 sp, dc in 3rd and 4th ch's of next sp, 2 dc in each of next 2 dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
18th rnd: Ch 3, dc in same place as sl st, 2 dc in each of next 3 dc, * dc in next 2 dc and in next 2 ch, ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 4, dc in 4th and 5th ch's of next sp, dc in next 2 dc, 2 dc in next 4 dc. Repeat , from * around. Join.
19th rnd: Ch 3, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, * dc in next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next 6 dc and in next 2 ch, ch 5, skip next dc, dc in next 5 dc, ch 5, dc in 3rd and 4th ch's of next sp, dc in next 6 dc, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
20th rnd: Ch 3, * dc in each dc to within last 5 dc of group, 2 dc in next dc, dc in next 4 dc and in next 2 ch, skip 1 dc, dc in next 3 dc, ch 4, dc in 3rd and 4th ch's of next sp, dc in next 4 dc, 2 dc in next dc. Repeat from * around. Join.
21st rnd: Ch 3, dc in each dc of group to within last 5 dc, 2 dc in next dc, * dc in next 2 ch, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in 3rd and 4th ch's of next sp, dc in next 4 dc of next group, 2 dc in next dc, dc in each dc to within last 5 dc, 2 dc in next dc, dc in each remaining dc of group. Repeat from * around. Join.
22nd rnd: Ch 3, dc in each dc of group, * dc in next 2 ch, ch 3, dc in 2nd and 3rd ch's of next sp, dc in each dc of next group. Repeat from * around. Join.
23rd rnd: Ch 3, dc in each dc and in each ch around. Join.
24th rnd: Ch 8, * skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 5. Repeat from * around. Join to 3rd ch of ch-8.
25th to 32nd rnds incl: Sl st to center of next sp, ch 8, * dc in next sp, ch 5. Repeat from * around. Join.
Break off at end of 32nd rnd.
Starch lightly and press.
