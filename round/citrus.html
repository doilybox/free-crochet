Golden Citrus Doily

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 2 balls Lt. Yellow or color of your choice.
Approximate size: 19 inches in diameter or The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 3 balls Yellow Spangle or color of your choice.
Approximate size: 21 inches in diameter.
Steel crochet hook No. 7.
1st Round: Chain (ch) 6, join to form a ring, ch 3, double crochet (dc) in ring, * ch 3, 2 dc in ring, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, join in 3rd stitch (st) of ch.
2nd Round: Ch 3, 2 dc in next dc, * ch 7, dc in next dc, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around, ch 7, join in 3rd st of ch.
3rd Round: Slip stitch (Sl st) into loop, ch 1 and work 5 single crochet (sc) in same space, * ch 7, 5 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, ch 7, join.
4th Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, 1 sc in each of the next 4 sc, * ch 2, 5 sc in next loop, ch 2, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sc, repeat from * all around ending with ch 2, 5 sc in next loop, ch 2, join.
5th Round: Sl st to next sc, ch 1, sc in same space, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sc, * ch 5, skip next ch 2 loop, 1 sc in each of the 3 center sc of next sc group, repeat from * all around, ch 5, join.
6th Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, 1 sc in each of the next 2 sc, * ch 6, 1 sc in each of the next 3 sc, repeat from * all around, ch 6, join.
7th Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, * ch 3, skip 1 sc, sc in next sc, ch 9, sc in next sc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
8th Round: Sl st into ch 3 loop, ch 9, dc in center st of next loop, * ch 5, treble crochet (trc) in next small loop, ch 5, dc in center st of next large loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 4th st of ch.
9th Round: Sl st to center st of next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 2, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc (shell) in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around ending with dc in 3rd st of ch 3 (this brings thread in position for next round) .
10th Round: Ch 4, 3 trc over last dc, * ch 5, double treble crochet ([d trc] 3 times over hook) over same dc, trc in center of next shell, trc in next ch 2 loop, trc in center of next shell, d trc in next ch 2 loop, ch 5, 4 trc in same loop, skip next shell, 4 trc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
11 th Round: Ch 4, keeping last loop of each trc on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 3 trc, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 7, skip next loop, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sts, ch 7, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc (4 trc cluster: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 4 sts, thread over and work off all loops at one time) , ch 5, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
12th Round: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 5, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sc, ch 5, 3 dc in top of next cluster, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in top of next cluster, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
13th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, * ch 4, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sc, ch 4, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sc in next ch 2 loop, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join.
14th Round: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, * 1 trc with ch 1 between each trc in each of the next 5 sc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
15th Round: Ch 4, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 2 dc, thread over and work off all loops at time, ** ch 5, skip 2 trc, sc in next trc, ch 5, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 3 dc, thread over and work off all loops at one (a 3 trc cluster) , * ch 4, dc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 4, 3 trc cluster over next 3 dc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
16th Round: Ch 5, ** dc in next cluster, * ch 5, trc in next dc, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, dc in next cluster, ch 2, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 3rd st of ch.
17th Round: Sl st into ch 2 loop, ch 8, ** skip next dc, trc in next trc, * ch 7, trc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 5, skip next dc, dc in next ch 2 loop, ch 5, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 3rd st of ch 8.
18th Round: Sl st to next trc, ch 1, sc in same space, * ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, sc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, skip next dc, sc in next trc, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 1st sc.
19th Round: Sl st to next ch 2 loop between next 2 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, skip next two ch 3 loops, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc (shell) in next ch 2 loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, skip next 3 loops, shell in next ch 2 loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch.
20th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 4 (counts as 1 trc) , d trc in next ch 3 loop, * ch 5, 4 trc in same loop, ch 1, skip next shell, 4 trc in next ch 3 loop, ch 5, d trc in same space, trc in center of next shell, trc in next loop, trc in center of next shell, d trc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 5, 4 trc in same loop, ch 1, skip 1 shell, 4 trc in next loop, ch 5, d trc in same space, trc in center of next shell, trc in next ch 3 loop, join in 4th st of ch.
21st Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, sc in next trc, * ch 7, skip 1 loop, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc, ch 7, 4 trc cluster over next 4 trc, ch 7, skip next loop, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sts, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
22nd Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, sc in next sc, * ch 5, 3 dc in top of next cluster, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in next cluster, ch 5, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
23rd Round: Ch 1, sc in same space, sc in next sc, * ch 4, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, skip next ch 2 loop, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 4, 1 sc in each of the next 5 sc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
24th Round: Sl st to next sc, ch 4, * 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 loop, dc in next ch 2 loop, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 2, 1 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, dc in next ch 2 loop, ch 5, skip next dc and next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 1 trc with ch 1 between each trc in each of the next 5 sc, repeat from * all around ending last repeat with 1 trc with ch 1 between each trc in each of the next 4 sc, join in 1st dc.
25th Round: Ch 4, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each of the next 2 dc, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 7, trc in next dc, ch 7, trc in next dc, ch 5, trc in next dc, ch 7, trc in next dc, ch 7, 3 trc cluster over next 3 dc, ch 3, skip next 5 trc, 3 trc cluster over next 3 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join.
26th Round: Ch 11, trc in next trc, * ch 8, trc in next trc, repeat from * twice, ch 8, dc in next cluster, dc in next cluster, ch 8, trc in next trc, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
27th Round: Ch 12, trc in next trc, * ch 9, trc in next trc, repeat from * twice, ** ch 9, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * ch 9, trc in next trc, repeat from * 3 times, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 9, join in 3rd st of ch.
28th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, * ch 4, 2 dc in next trc, ch 3, sl st in top of last dc for picot, dc in same space, ch 4, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, sc in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
