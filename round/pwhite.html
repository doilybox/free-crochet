Pink White Doily

MATERIALS:
Small, Medium and Large: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON 1 Ball each White and Shaded Lt. Pinks
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7
Approximate Size: Small doily: 6 inches in diameter. Medium doily: 9 1/2 inches in diameter. Large doily: 13 1/2 inches in diameter.
LARGE DOILY: With Shaded Lt. Pinks ch 7, join to form a ring, ch 1, 16 sc in ring, join in 1st sc.
2nd ROUND: * Ch 3, skip next sc, sl st in next sc, repeat from * all around, ch 3, sl st in joining.
3rd ROUND: Sl st into loop, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 1, trc in joining (this brings thread in position for next round).
4th ROUND: 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc over trc, * ch 5, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 2, dc in trc.
5th ROUND: 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in dc, * ch 5, skip next loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc (picot group) in center st of next ch 5 loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
6th ROUND: Attach White in any ch 5 loop, sc in same loop, * ch 11, sc in next ch 5 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 11, join in 1st sc.
7th ROUND: Sl st into loop, sc in same loop, * ch 3, sc in same loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around, ch 3, join in 1st sc.
8th ROUND: Sl st into next loop, ch 6, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next loop, ** ch 3, keeping last loop of each st on hook dc in next loop, skip next loop, dc in next loop, thread over and work off all loops at one time (2 dc cluster st), * ch 3, dc in next loop, repeat from *twice, repeat from ** all around ending with ch 3, dc in next loop, join in 3rd st of ch.
9th ROUND: Sl st into next loop, ch 1, sc in same loop, * ch 7, skip next dc, sc in next dc, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending last repeat with ch 2, dc in ,1st sc.
10th ROUND: Ch 3, 2 dc in dc, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
11th ROUND: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, skip next dc, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
12 ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, * 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next loop, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
13th ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, * 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next loop, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
14th ROUND: Ch 3, keeping last loop of each st on hook, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, thread over and work off all loops at one time (all 1st cluster sts will be worked in same manner), * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, cluster st over next 5 dc (cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, thread over and work off all loops at one time), ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, sc in next loop, ch 5, cluster st over next 5 dc, repeat from * all around ending last repeat with ch 2, dc in 1st cluster st.
15 ROUND: * Ch 7, sc in next loop, repete from * three times, ch 7, skip next picot, sc in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending last repeat with ch 3, trc in dc.
16t ROUND: Ch 3,2 dc in same space, ** ch 5, sc in next loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, 3 dc in center st of next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
17th ROUND: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, skip next dc, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
18 ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, * dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next dc, 3 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next loop, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
19th ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, * 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, sc in next loop, ch 3, 2 dc in next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, sc in next loop, ch 3, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
20th ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, * 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next 3 loops, 2 dc in next dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of next 3 dc, 2 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next 3 loops, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
21st ROUND: Work a cluster st over the 1st 6 sts, * ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, cluster st over next 6 dc, ch 7, cluster st over next 5 dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, cluster st over next 5 dc, ch 7, cluster st over next 6 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st, cut thread.
22nd ROUND: Attach Shaded Lt. Pinks in center st of any ch 7 loop, picot group in same space, * ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next dc, ch 3, skip next loop, picot group in center st of next loop, ch 3, skip next loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next dc, ch 3, skip next loop, picot group in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc, cut thread.
23rd ROUND: Attach White in center picot of any picot group, sc in same space, * ch 13, sc in center picot of next picot group, repeat from * all around, join in 1st sc.
24th and 25 ROUNDS: Same as 7th and 8th rounds.
26th ROUND: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc in next cluster st, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
27 ROUND: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * ch 3, skip next dc, 3 dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
28th ROUND: Ch 3, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, 2 dc in next dc, skip next 2 loops, 2 dc in next dc, repeat from * all around endding to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
29th ROUND: Ch 3, cluster st over the next 3 sts, * ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, sc in next loop, ch 4, cluster st over next 4 sts, ch 1, cluster st over next 4 sts, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st, cut thread.
30th ROUND: Attach Shaded Lt. Pinks in any ch 1 space, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, picot group (3 picots) in next ch 1 space, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
31st ROUND: Sl st to center picot, * ch 6, skip next picot and next 2 loops, picot group in next loop, ch 6, skip next 2 loops and next picot, sl st in next picot, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
MEDIUM DOILY Work from 1st to 22nd rounds of large doily.
SMALL DOILY Work from 1st to 13th rounds of large doily.
14th ROUND: Work a cluster over 1st 5 sts, * ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in picot, ch 3, 1 dc in next sc, ch 3, cluster over next 5 dc, ch 7, cluster over next 5 dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
15th ROUND: Work same as 22nd round of large doily.
