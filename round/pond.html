The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40, 3 balls Silver Spangle or The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40, 2. balls White.
Steel Hook No. 7.
Approx.size: 16 inches in diameter for "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED, 15 inches in diameter for "PURITAN".
1st Round: With Silver Spangle or White chain (ch) 10, join to form a ring, ch 1, single crochet (sc) in ring, * ch 5, sc in ring, repeat from * 10 times, ch 2, double crochet (dc) in 1st sc (this brings thread in position for next round).
2nd Round: Ch 4, keeping. last loop of each stitch (st) on hook work 3 treble crochet (trc) in top of dc, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 5, cluster st in center st of next loop (cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 4 trc in same st, thread over and work off all loops at one time), repeat from * 10 times, ch 5, join.
3rd Round: Ch 1, work 3 sc, ch 3, 3 sc in each loop, join in 1st sc.
4th Round: Slip stitch (sl st) to next loop, ch 4 (always counts as part of 1st cluster st), keeping last loop of each st on hook work 3 trc in same loop, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 11, cluster st in same loop, * ch 3, 2 cluster sts with ch 9 between in next loop, ch 3, 2 cluster sts with ch 11 between in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
