Autumn Wheat Free Crochet Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40, 2 balls White, Cream or Ecru or The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40, 6 balls Silver Spangle or color of your choice.
Approx. size: 22 inches in diameter for "PURITAN"; 26 1/2 inches in diameter for "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7
1st Round: Chain (ch) 10, join to form a ring, ch 4 (counts as 1st treble crochet [trc] of 1st cluster), keeping last loop of each stitch (st) on hook work 3 trc in ring, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 4, 4 trc cluster st in ring (4 trc cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 4 trc in same space, thread over and work off all loops at one time), repeat from * 6 times, ch 4, join in top of 1st cluster.
2nd Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st (to complete cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 3 trc in same space, thread over and work off all loops at one time), * ch 4, trc, in next loop, ch 4, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from * 6 times, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, join in 1st cluster st.
3rd Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, * ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 3, join.
4th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, ** ch 3, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from ** 6 times, ch 3, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, join.
5th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, ** ch 3, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
6th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, ** ch 3, skip next loop, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, ch 15, 4 trc cluster st in same cluster st, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
7th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, * ch 3, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in same loop, ch 2, 2 trc cluster st in same loop (2 trc cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 2 trc in same space, thread over and work off all loops at one time), ch 2, trc in same loop, ch 4, trc in same loop, ch 4, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
8th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, * ch 3, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, ch 3, trc in next loop, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, 3 trc in next cluster st, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
9th Round: Ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster st, ** 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, ch 3, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 2, 3 trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, 3, trc in next trc, ch 2, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 3, 4 trc cluster st in next cluster st, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
10th Round: Slip stitch (sl st) to center of next loop, ch 8, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * once, ** skip next trc, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
11th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ** ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, * ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, repeat from * once, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * once, one-2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
12th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, ** trc in next loop, ch 4, skip next loop, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 3, trc in last trc already worked in, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 3, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, * ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, repeat from * once, ch 3, trc in last trc worked in, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 3, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
13th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 6, ** trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, trc in next trc, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 2, trc in next loop, trc in next trc cluster, trc in next loop, ch 2, trc in next loop, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, trc in next trc, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 2, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
14th Round: Ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ** ch 4, one 3 trc cluster over next 3 sts,* ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from *' once, ch 2, skip next trc and next loop, 3 trc in next trc, ch 2, 2 trc in next trc, ch 2, 3 trc in next trc, ch 2, skip next loop, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * once, one 3 trc cluster over next 3 sts, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, keeping last loop of the following 3 sts on hook work 1 trc in next loop, 1 trc in next 2 trc cluster, skip next loop, 1 trc in next loop, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
15th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 3 times, ** skip next trc, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, ch 4, skip next loop, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 3 times, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 3 times, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
16th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, ** trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 3 trc in next trc, * 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, repeat from * once, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 3 times, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
17th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * twice, ** skip next loop, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, trc in last trc already worked in, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, * ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, repeat from * once, ch 2, trc in last trc already worked in, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 4, skip next loop, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * twice, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
18th Round: Sl st in next loop, ch 4 and complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, * 1 trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * twice, trc in next loop, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, trc in next trc, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, * ch 2, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, trc in next trc cluster, * trc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * once, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, trc in next trc, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 3 times, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in top of 1st cluster.
19th Round: Sl st in next loop, ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * twice, * trc in next loop, ch 2, repeat from * once, one 3 trc cluster over next 3 sts, ch 2, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 2, skip next trc and next loop, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, ch 2, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 2, one 3 trc cluster over next 3 sts, * ch 2, trc in next loop, repeat from * once, ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * twice, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
20th ROUND: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, ** trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 4, skip next trc and next loop, trc in next trc cluster, ch 4, skip next loop, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next loop, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * once, trc in next trc cluster, ch 4, skip next loop), * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat front * 3 times, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
21st Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, ** trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 4, skip next trc and next loop, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, trc in last trc already worked in, trc in next trc, 3 trc in next trc, 1 trc in each of the next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 4, skip next loop, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 5 times, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
22nd Round: Sl st in next loop, ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 5 times, trc in next loop, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, trc in next trc, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 trc, ch 2, one 2 trc cluster over last trc already worked in and next trc, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 6 times, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond join.
23rd Round: Sl st in next loop, ch 4 and complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 6 times, trc in next loop, ch 2, one 3 trc cluster over next 3 sts, ch 2, *trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 7 times, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
24th Round: Ch 8, ** trc in next loop, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ch 2, trc in next cluster, ch 2, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 8 times, trc between next 2 clusters, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
25th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, ch 8, ** trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, trc in next loop, * ch 5, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, repeat from * once, ch 5, trc in next loop, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, trc in next trc, * ch 4, trc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, one 2 trc cluster over next 2 loops, ch 4, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
26th Round: Sl st to next loop, ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 4 times, trc in next trc, ch 4, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
27th Round: Sl st to next loop, ch 4, complete 4 trc cluster, ** ch 4, * trc in next loop, ch 4, repeat from * 5 times, one 4 trc cluster in each of the next 2 loops, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
28th Round: Ch 4, sl st in same space for picot, ** ch 4, double crochet (dc) in next trc, ch 4, sl st in top of dc for picot, * ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, sl st in top of trc for picot, repeat from * 3 times, ch 4, dc in next trc, ch 4, sl st in top of dc for picot, ch 4, single crochet (sc) between next two 4 trc clusters, ch 4, sl st in sc for picot, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
