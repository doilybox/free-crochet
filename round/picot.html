Picot Doily

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON 1 Ball White.
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7
Approximate Size: 13 inches in diameter
1st ROUND: * Ch 5, sl st in 5th st from hook for picot, repeat from * 7 times, join.
2nd ROUND: Sl st to 1st picot, * ch 7, sl st in 5th st from hook for picot, repeat from * 3 times, ch 2, dc in 2nd picot from hook, ch 5, sl st in top of dc for picot, * ch 2, dc in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of dc for picot, repeat from * once, ch 2, sc in next picot, repeat from 1st * all around, join, cut thread.
3rd ROUND: Attach thread in center picot of any point, ch 7, tr in same space, * ch 3, tr in same space, repeat from * twice, * ch 5, 5 tr with ch 3 between each tr in center picot of next point, repeat from last * all around, ch 5, join in 4th st of ch.
4th ROUND: Sl st to loop, ch 4 * thread over twice, insert in loop, pull through and work off two loops twice, repeat from * 3 times, thread over and work off all loops, ** ch 7, work a 5 tr cluster st in next loop (5 tr cluster st: * thread over twice, insert in loop, pull through and work off 2 loops twice, repeat from * 4 times, thread over and work off all loops), repeat from ** twice, ch 1, skip 1 loop, 5 tr cluster st in next loop, complete round to correspond, join.
5th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, * work 3 picots same as 2nd round (picot, ch 7, sl st in 5th st from hook), ch 2, dc in 2nd picot from hook, ch 5, sl st in top of dc for picot, ch 2, tr in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of tr, ch 2, sc in next ch 5 loop, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.
6th ROUND: Attach thread in center picot of any point, * work 4 picots, ch 2, dc in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of dc , ch 2, tr in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of tr, ch 2, dtr (3 times over hook) in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of dtr, ch 2, sc in next center picot of next point, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.
7th ROUND: Attach thread in center picot of any point, ch 9, tr in same space, * ch 5, tr in same space, repeat from * twice, * ch 2, 5 tr with ch 5 between in center picot of next point, repeat from last * all around, join in 4th st of ch.
8th ROUND: Sl st to loop, ch 1, sc in same loop, * ch 6, 5 tr cluster st in next loop, ch 5, tr in next tr, ch 7, tr in same space, ch 5, 5 tr cluster st in next loop, ch 6, sc in next loop, sl st in ch 2 loop, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond.
9th ROUND: Sl st to ch 5 loop, * ch 7, work three 5 tr cluster sts with ch 7 between each cluster st in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 2 skip next 2 loops, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
10th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, * work 2 picots, ch 2, dc in 2nd picot from hook, ch 5, sl st in top of dc , ch 2 sc in next loop, work 3 picots, ch 2, dc in 2nd picot from hook, ch 5, sl st in top of dc , ch 2, tr in next picot, ch 5, sl st in top of tr, ch 2, sc in next loop, work 2 picots, ch 2, dc in 2nd picot from hook, ch 5, sl st in top of dc , ch 2, sc in next loop, ch 2, sc in next ch 7 loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
