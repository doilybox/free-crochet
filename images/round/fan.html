Shell Fan Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON or "GIANT" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON 1 Ball White
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7
Approximate Size: 14 inches in diameter
1st ROUND: * Ch 4, dc in 4th st from hook (rice st), repeat from * 5 times, join to 1st rice st.
2nd ROUND: Ch 4, tr in same space leaving last loop of each st on hook, thread over hook and pull through all loops at one time, ch 5, 2 cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st in same space (cluster st: 3 tr in same space leaving last loop of each st on hook, thread over hook and pull through all loops at one time), * ch 2, 3 cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st between next 2 rice sts, repeat from * 4 times, ch 2, join in 1st cluster st.
3rd ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 8, dc in same space, ch 1, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ** ch 1, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc between next 2 cluster sts, * ch 1, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from * once, then repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
4th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 3, skip next loop, dc in next loop, * ch 5, skip next ch 1 space, 5 tr in next loop, ch 5, skip next ch 1 space, dc in next loop, skip next ch 1 space, dc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join in 3rd st of ch.
5th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 8, * 5 dc in next tr, skip next tr, sc in next tr, skip next tr, 5 dc in next tr, ch 5, dc in next loop, dc in next loop, ch 5, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
6th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 8, * 5 dc in next dc, skip next dc, sc in next dc, skip next 2 dc, 5 dc in sc, skip next 2 dc, sc in next dc, skip next dc, 5 dc in next dc, ch 5, dc in next loop, dc in next loop, ch 5, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
7th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 2, sc in same loop, ** 5 dc in next dc, skip next dc, sc in next dc, * skip 2 dc, 5 dc in next sc, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, repeat from * once, skip 1 dc, 5 dc in next dc, sc in next loop, ch 1, sc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 1st loop.
8th ROUND: Ch, 6, dc in same space, **ch 3, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc,*5 dc in next sc, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, repeat from * twice, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, in next ch 1 space, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
9th ROUND: Sl st into loop, ch 6, dc in same space, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in center of dc of next dc group, * 5 dc in next sc, sc in center dc of next dc group, repeat from * once, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
10th ROUND: Sl st into center of loop, ch 6, dc in same space, ch 3, dc in next dc, * ch 3, sc in next ch 5 loop, ch 5, sc in center dc of next dc group, 5 dc in next sc, sc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
11th ROUND: Sl st to loop, 5 cluster sts with ch 3 between each cluster st in same space, * ch 5, skip next dc, 3 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next loop, 5 cluster sts with ch 3 between each cluster st in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st.
12th ROUND: Sl st to loop, ch 6, dc in same space, * ch 2, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc, in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next 2 loops, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 2 loops, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next ch 3 loop between next 2 cluster sts, repeat from 1st * all around ending to corres ond, join in 3rd st of ch.
13th ROUND: Sl st into loop, 2 cluster sts with c 5 between in same space, * ch 4, skip next loop, 2 cluster sts with ch 5 between in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 2 cluster sts with ch 5 between in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join.
14th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 8, dc in same space, * ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip 1 loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc between next 2 cluster sts, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
15th ROUND: Sl st to loop, 3 cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st in same space, * ch 2, skip next loop, 3 cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 3 cluster sts with ch 5 between each cluster st in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join.
16th ROUND: Sl st to center of loop, ch 8, dc in same space, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, * ch 3, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next ch 5 loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, 3 dc in center dc of 3 dc group, ch 5, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 5, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
17th ROUND: Sl st to loop, ch 3, 2 dc, 3 tr, 3 dc in same loop, * skip ch 3 loop, 3 dc, 3 tr, 3 dc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 3, skip next loop, 1 dc, ch 5, 1 dc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc in center dc of next dc group, ch 3, skip next loop, 3 dc, 3 tr, 3 dc in next loop, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
18th ROUND: Sl st to center tr, ch 5, sc in same space, * ch 5, skip next tr and 3 dc, sc between next 2 dc, ch 5, sc in next center tr, ch 5, sc in same space, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, work 1 sc in next 2 loops (to work 1 sc in 2 loops: insert hook in loop, pull thread through, insert in next loop, pull thread through, thread over hook and pull through all loops at one time), ch 4, 1 sc, ch 5, 1 sc picot in next loop, ch 4, work 1 sc in next 2 loops, ch 5, 1 sc, ch 5, 1 sc in center tr of next group, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
