The Big Colorful Throw Crochet Pattern

MATERIALS: AMERICAN THREAD "AUNT LYDIA'S" HEAVY RUG YARN 1—70 yd. skein Yellow 2—70 yd. skeinschartreuse 4—70 yd. skeins Orange 12—70 yd. skeins White 24—70 yd. skeins assortedcolors and "AUNT LYDIA'S" CARPET AND BUTTON THREAD 1 spool White Aluminum Crochet Hook Size H OR ANY SIZE HOOK WHICH WILL RESULT IN STITCH GAUGE BELOW GAUGE: 3 dc = 1 inch
MOTIF: With Yellow ch 4, join to form a ring.
1st Rnd: Ch 1, work 8 sc in ring, join in 1st sc, cut yarn.
2nd Rnd: Attach Orange in same st, ch 3, 4 dc in same st, drop loop from hook, insert hook in 3rd st of ch, pick up dropped loop and pull through, * ch 2, 5 dc in next sc, drop loop, insert hook in 1st dc, pick up loop and pull through (popcorn st), repeat from * 6 times, ch 2, join in top of 1st popcorn st, cut Orange.
3rd Rnd: Attach any color in a ch 2 loop, ch 3, 4 dc in same loop, * 5 dc in next loop, repeat from * all aRnd, join in 3rd st of ch.
4th Rnd: Ch 3, 1 dc in next st, 3 dc in next st, * 1 dc in each of next 4 sts, 3 dc in next st, repeat from * all aRnd ending with 1 dc in each of next 2 sts, join.
5th Rnd: Ch 3, 1 dc in each st, join.
6th Rnd: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of next 2 sts, 3 dc in next st, * 1 dc in each of next 6 sts, 3 dc in next st, repeat from * all around ending with 1 dc in each of the next 3 sts, join.
7th Rnd: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of next 3 sts, 3 dc in next st, * 1 dc in each of next 8 sts, 3 dc in next st, repeat from * all around ending 1 dc in each of next 4 sts, join.
8th Rnd: Ch 3, 1 dc in each of next 4 sts, 3 dc in next st, * 1 dc in each of next 10 sts, 3 dc in next st, repeat from * all around ending 1 dc in each of next 5 sts, join, cut yarn.
9th Rnd: Attach White, work 1 s c in each st and 3 sc in each corner st, join, cut yarn.
Work 23 more motifs working from 3rd to 8th Rnds in assorted colors.
JOINING MOTIFS: With Yellow work 1st Rnd colors same as Motif.
2nd Rnd: Attach Chartreuse and work same as 2nd Rnd of motif.
3rd Rnd: Attach White in any ch 2 loop, ch 4, 1 tr c, 5 dc in same loop, * 5 dc, 2 tr c in next loop, 2 tr c, 5 dc in next loop, repeat from * twice, 5 dc, 2 tr c in next loop, join, cut yarn. Work 14 more joining motifs to correspond.
FINISHING: Sew motifs tog through back loops of sts, and sew joining motifs in each space as illustrated.
POMPONS: Open skein of White, cut one end. Cut Carpet and But­ton Thread into 18 inch lengths, fold thread in half, and tie skein tightly at 1 inch intervals. Cut between tying. Shape and trim pompons. Attach 1 pompon to each point of all motifs and tie 2 pompons spaced evenly between all points.
PILLOWS
Materials Required:
AMERICAN THREAD "AUNT LYDIA'S" HEAVY RUG YARN
1—70 yd. skein each Orange and Yellow
2—70 yd. skeins White
8—70 yd. skeins assortedcolors will make 4 pillows
Kapok or shredded foam rubber

Work 2 motifs of any 2 main col­ors same as bedspread. Sew motifs tog leaving an opening. Fill with kapok or shredded foam rubber. Sew opening closed. Fin­ish with pompons or if desired omit pompons.

NOTE: I think you could use any yarn if you modify the pattern a little and just add more crochet blocks or add extra rows.
