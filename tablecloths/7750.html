Free Vintage Crochet - 7750 Tablecloth Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:
J. & P. Coats or Clarks O.N.T. Best Six Cord Mercerized Crochet , Size 50 (white only). Small Ball: J. & P. Coats - 59 balls or Clarks O.N.T. - 95 balls.
Steel Hook Size #12
Finished size: 61" x 81"

GAUGE: Motif measures 2 1/4 inches in diameter.

FIRST MOTIF . . . Starting at center, ch 12. Join with sl st to form ring.
1st rnd: Ch 4, 31 tr in ring. Sl st in top of ch-4.
2nd rnd: Ch 4, 2 dc in first st of ch-4, * skip 1 tr, tr in next tr, 2 dc in post of tr just made. Repeat from * around (16 groups). Join.
3rd rnd: Ch 4, holding back on hook the last loop of each dc make dc in next 2 dc, (thread over and draw through 2 loops) twice; * ch 5, holding back on hook the last loop of each dc make dc in next tr and the following 2 dc, thread over and draw through 2 loops at a time 3 times (cluster made). Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2, dc in tip of 1st cluster.
4th rnd: * Ch 7, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3, tr in dc.
5th rnd: * Ch 9, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with sl st in tr. Break off.

SECOND MOTIF . . . Work as for First Motif until 4 rnds are completed.
5th rnd: Ch 4, sc in loop of First Motif, ch 4, sc in next loop on Second Motif, (ch 4, sc in next loop on First Motif, ch 4, sc in next loop on Second Motif) twice; and complete as for First Motif (no more joinings). Make 27 rows of 36 motifs, joining as Second Motif was joined to First Motif, leaving 1 loop free on each motif between joinings.

FILL-IN-MOTIF . . . Ch 12. Join to form ring. Ch 4, 7 tr in ring, sl st in free ch-9 loop (between joinings) ; (8 tr in ring, sl st in next free ch-9 loop) 3 times. SI st in top of ch-4. Break off.
EDGING . . . Attach thread to joining preceding corner motif, 4 sc in next loop, (in next loop make 4 sc, ch 4 and 4 sc) 9 times; * (4 sc in next loop) twice; (in next loop make 4 sc, ch 4 and 4 sc) 5 times. Repeat from * around, working over corner motifs as before. Join and break off.
