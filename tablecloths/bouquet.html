Bride's Bouquet Free Crochet Tablecloth Pattern
MATERIALS:
J. & P. COATS "KNIT-CRO-SHEEN," Art. A. 64: 19 balls of No. 1 White, No. 61 Ecru No. 42 Cream;
or
J. & P. COATS "KNIT-CRO-SHEEN," Art. A. 65: 12 balls of No. 1 White or No. 61 Ecru.
Milwards Steel Crochet Hook NO. 7.
Starting at center Ch 8. Join with sl st to form ring.
1st rnd: Ch 3, make 15 dc in ring. Join to top of ch-3.
2nd rnd: Ch 3, skip joining, * dc in next dc, ch 4, sl st in 4th ch from hook—picot made; dc in same dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, making dc, picot and dc in last dc. Join to top of ch-3.
3rd rnd: Ch 23, * dc in 8th ch from hook, (ch 2, skip 2 ch, dc in next ch) 3 times; ch 2, skip 2 ch, dtr in next ch; skip next dc, next picot and following dc of 2nd rnd, dc in next dc—spoke started; ch 20. Repeat from * around, ending with dtr in last ch of last spoke. Join to 3rd ch of ch-23 (where first dtr was made)-8 spokes.
4th rnd: * In first sp of next spoke make 2 sc and hdc; (4 dc in next sp of same spoke) 3 times; 10 dc in next sp; working along opposite side of same spoke, (make 4 dc in next sp) 3 times; in next sp make hdc and 2 sc. Repeat from * around. Join to first sc.
5th rnd: Sl st in next sc, following hdc and next 6 dc; ch 3, dc in next 8 dc, * (2 dc in next dc) 6 times; dc in next 9 dc, skip last 8 sts of this spoke and first 8 sts of next spoke, dc in next 9 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with (2 dc in next dc) 6 times; dc in next 9 dc. Join to top of ch-3.
6th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 7 dc, ch 5, skip next dc, * (dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc) twice; (in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc; ch 2, skip 1 dc) twice; dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 3, skip first 7 dc of next spoke, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc. Repeat from * around, ending with (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 3. Join to 3rd ch of ch-5.
7th rnd: Ch 5, (dc in next dc, ch 2) twice; * in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 2, dc in next dc) twice; ch 2, in next dc make dc, ch 2 and dc; (ch 2, dc in next dc) 6 times; ch 2. Repeat from * around, ending with (ch 2, dc in next dc) 3 times; ch 2. Join as on 6th rnd.
8th rnd: Ch 5, * dc in next dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around. Join as on 6th rnd.
9th rnd: In first sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) 10 times; * skip next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 11 times. Repeat from * around, ending with skip last sp. Join to top of ch-3.
10th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 12 times; * ch 20, dc in 8th ch from hook, (ch 2, skip 2 ch, dc in next ch) 4 times—spoke started; skip next 6 dc of 9th rnd, dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 13 times. Repeat from * around, ending with dc in last ch of last spoke. Join as on 6th rnd-8 spokes.
11th rnd: * In next sp make sc and hdc; (2 dc in next sp) 11 times; in next sp make hdc and sc; in first sp of next spoke make 2 sc and hdc; (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; 10 dc in next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; in next sp make hdc and 2 sc. Repeat from * around. Join to first sc.
12th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 5 sts, ch 5, * skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 7 times; skip first 5 sts of next spoke, dc in next 9 dc, (2 dc in next dc). 6 times; dc in next 9 dc, skip next 10 sts, dc in next dc, ch 2. Repeat from * around, ending with skip last 10 sts. Join as on 6th rnd.
13th rnd: Sl st across first sp, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 5 times; ch 3, skip next sp and following 4 dc, sc in next dc, (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) twice; (ch 5, skip 1 dc, sc in next dc) twice; ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc, (ch 5, skip 1 dc, sc in next dc) twice; (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) twice; ch 3, skip next sp, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join to top of ch-3.
14th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 5 times; ch 4, skip next sp, (sc in next loop, ch 5) 4 times; in next loop make sc, ch 5 and sc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 4 times; ch 4, skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4. Join as on 6th rnd.
15th rnd: Sl st across first sp, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; ch 5, skip 2 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 8 times; ch 5, skip 2 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join to top of ch-3.
16th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 7, skip 1 sp, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 7 times; ch 7, skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 7. Join as on 6th rnd.
17th rnd: Sl st across first sp, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * ch 11, skip 2 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 7, sc in next loop) 6 times; ch 11, skip 2 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 11. Join to top of ch-3.
18th rnd: Ch 5, skip joining and next dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 3, skip 3 ch, dc in next ch) twice; ch 5, sc in next loop, (ch 7, sc in next loop) 5 times; ch 5, skip first 3 ch of next sp, (dc in next ch, ch 3, skip 3 ch) twice; dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join as on 6th rnd.
19th rnd: In first sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) twice; * (5 dc in next sp) twice; (7 dc in next loop) 3 times; (5 dc in next sp) twice; (3 dc in next sp) 5 times. Repeat from * around, ending with 3 dc in each of last 2 sps. Join to top of ch-3.
20th rnd: Ch 1, skip joining, sc in next dc, (ch 7, skip 4 dc, sc in next dc) 5 times; * (ch 7, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) twice; (ch 7, skip 4 dc, sc in next dc) 10 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3, tr in first sc to form last loop.
21st rnd: Ch 1, sc in loop just formed, * ch 7, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending as on 20th rnd.
22nd through 26th rnd: Ch 1, sc in loop just formed, * ch 8, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4, tr in first sc.
27th through 30th rnd: Ch 1, sc in loop just formed, * ch 9, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4, dtr in first sc.
31st rnd: Ch 7, in loop just formed make tr, ch 3 and tr; * ch 3, in next loop make dc, ch 3 and dc; (ch 3, in next loop make sc, ch 3 and sc) twice; ch 3, in next loop make dc, ch 3 and dc; (ch 3, in next loop make tr, ch 3, tr, ch 3 and tr) twice. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join to 4th ch of ch-7.
32nd rnd: In next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) 5 times; * skip next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 13 times. Repeat from * around, ending with skip next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 7 times. Join to top of ch-3.
33rd rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 21 dc (these 21 dc will be used again to complete last repeat); ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 15 times; ch 23, dc in 8th ch from hook, (ch 2, skip 2 ch, dc in next ch) 5 times-spoke started; skip next 6 dc of 32nd rnd, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with dc in last ch of last spoke. Join as on 6th rnd-16 spokes.
34th rnd: * In next sp make sc and hdc; (2 dc in next sp) 5 times; (3 dc in next sp) 4 times; (2 dc in next sp) 5 times; in next sp make hdc and sc; in first sp of next spoke make sc and 2 hdc; (3 dc in next sp) 4 times; 10 dc in next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 4 times; in last sp of same spoke make 2 hdc and sc. Repeat from * around. Join to first sc.
35th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 5 sts, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 12 times; skip first 6 sts of next spoke, dc in next 11 dc, (2 dc in next dc) 6 times; dc in next 11 dc, skip next 11 sts, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with skip 11 sts. Join as on 6th rnd.
36th rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 8 times; ch 3, skip first 3 dc of next spoke, sc in next dc, (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) 3 times; (ch 5, skip 1 dc, sc in next dc) 5 times; (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) 3 times; ch 3, skip next 2 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. End and join as for 13th rnd.
37th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 9 times; ch 4, skip next sp, (sc in next loop, ch 5) 5 times; in next loop make sc, ch 5 and sc; (ch 5, sc in next loop) 5 times; ch 4, skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4. Join as on 6th rnd.
38th rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 5 times; ch 5, skip next 3 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 10 times; ch 5, skip next 3 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around. End and join as for 15th rnd.
39th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 5 times; ch 6, skip next sp, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 9 times; ch 6, skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 6. Join as on 6th rnd.
40th rnd: Sl st across first sp, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; ch 7, skip next 2 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 7, sc in next loop) 8 times; ch 7, skip next 2 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 7. Join to top of ch-3.
41st rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 7, skip next sp, (sc in next loop, ch 7) 8 times; skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 7. Join as on 6th rnd.
42nd through 45th rnd: Repeat 17th through 20th rnd.
Next 3 rnds: Repeat 21st rnd.
Next 13 rnds: Repeat 22nd rnd.
62nd rnd: Ch 6, dc in loop just formed, (ch 3, in next loop make dc, ch 3 and dc) twice; * ch 3, in next loop make tr, ch 3 and tr; ch 3, in next loop make (tr, ch 3) twice and tr; ch 3, in next loop make tr, ch 3 and tr; (ch 3, in next loop make dc, ch 3 and dc) 3 times; (ch 3, in next loop make sc, ch 3 and sc) 3 times; (ch 3, in next loop make dc, ch 3 and dc) 3 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join to 3rd ch of ch-6.
63rd rnd: In first sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) 19 times; * in next sp make hdc and 2 sc; sc in next sp, in next sp make 2 sc and hdc; (3 dc in next sp) 22 times. Repeat from * around. End and join as for 19th rnd.
64th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 27 times; * ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next hdc, ch 30, dc in 9th ch from hook, (ch 2, skip 2 ch, dc in next ch) 5 times; (ch 2, skip 2 ch, tr in next ch) twice-spoke started; skip next 6 sts of 63rd rnd, dc in next st, ch 2, skip 2 dc, dc in next dc, (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 30 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2. Join as on 6th rnd-16 spokes.
65th rnd: In first sp make sl st, ch 3 and dc; (2 dc in next sp) 27 times; * in next sp make hdc and sc; in first sp of next spoke make 2 sc and hdc; (3 dc in next sp) 6 times; 10 dc in next sp, (3 dc in next sp) 6 times; in next sp make hdc and 2 sc; in next sp make sc and hdc; (2 dc in next sp) 30 times. Repeat from * around, ending with 2 dc in each of last 2 sps. Join to top of ch-3.
66th rnd: Ch 5, skip joining and next dc; dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 25 times; skip 11 sts, dc in next 17 dc of next spoke, (2 dc in next dc) 6 times; dc in next 17 dc, skip next 12 sts, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with skip last 12 sts. Join as on 6th rnd.
67th rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and dc; (2 dc in next sp) 6 times; * (3 dc in next sp) 8 times; (2 dc in next sp) 7 times; ch 5, skip first 3 sts of next spoke, sc in next dc, (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) 5 times; (ch 5, skip 1 dc, sc in next dc) 5 times; (ch 5, skip 2 dc, sc in next dc) 5 times; ch 5, skip next 2 sps, (2 dc in next sp) 7 times. Repeat from * around. End and join as for 15th rnd.
68th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 4 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 21 times; ch 5, skip next sp, sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 14 times; ch 5, skip next sp and following 4 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join as on 6th rnd.
69th rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) twice; * (2 dc in next sp) 12 times; (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; ch 6, skip next 3 sps, (sc in next loop, ch 5) 6 times; (in next loop make sc, ch 5 and sc; ch 5) twice; sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 5 times; ch 6, skip next 3 sps, (3 dc in next sp) 3 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 6. Join to top of ch-3.
70th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 4 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 16 times; ch 6, skip next sp, sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 14 times; ch 6, skip next sp and following 4 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 6. Join as on 6th rnd.
71st rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) twice; * (2 dc in next sp) 7 times; (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; ch 7, skip next 3 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 5, sc in next loop) 13 times; ch 7, skip next 3 sps, (3 dc in next sp) 3 times. Repeat from * around. End and join as for 40th rnd.
72nd rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 4 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 11 times; ch 7, skip next sp, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 12 times; ch 7, skip next sp and following 4 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 7. Join as on 6th rnd.
73rd rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * (3 dc in next sp) 7 times; ch 8, skip next 3 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 6, sc in next loop) 11 times; ch 8, skip next 3 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 8. Join to top of ch-3.
74th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 4 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) 7 times; ch 10, skip next sp, sc in next loop, ch 7, in next loop make sc, ch 7 and sc; (ch 7, sc in next loop) twice; ch 7, in next loop make sc, ch 7 and sc; ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, in next loop make sc, ch 7 and sc; (ch 7, sc in next loop) twice; ch 7, in next loop make sc, ch 7 and sc; ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 10, skip next sp and following 4 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 10. Join as on 6th rnd.
75th rnd: Sl st across first 2 sps, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; (3 dc in next sp) 3 times; * ch 12, skip next 3 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 7, sc in next loop) 13 times; ch 12, skip next 3 sps, (3 dc in next sp) 4 times. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 12. Join to top of ch-3.
76th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next 3 dc, ch 5, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc) twice; ch 14, skip next sp, sc in next loop, (ch 8, sc in next loop) 12 times; ch 14, skip next sp and following 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 14. Join as on 6th rnd.
77th rnd: Sl st across first sp, in next sp make sl st, ch 3 and 2 dc; * ch 17, skip next 2 sps, sc in next loop, (ch 8, sc in next loop) 11 times; ch 17, skip next 2 sps, 3 dc in next sp. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 17. Join to top of ch-3.
78th rnd: Ch 5, skip joining and next dc, dc in next dc, * (ch 3, skip 3 ch, dc in next ch) 4 times; ch 3, sc in next loop, (ch 8, sc in next loop) 10 times; ch 3, skip first ch of next sp, dc in next ch, (ch 3, skip 3 ch, dc in next ch) 3 times; ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join as on 6th rnd.
79th rnd: In first sp make sl st, ch 3 and 3 dc; (4 dc in next sp) 5 times; * (8 dc in next sp) 10 times; (4 dc in next sp) 11 times. Repeat from * around. Join to top of ch-3.
80th rnd: Skip joining, sl st in next dc, sc in next dc, (ch 7, skip 4 dc, sc in next dc) 4 times; * (ch 7, skip 5 dc, sc in next dc) 14 times; (ch 7, skip 4 dc, sc in next dc) 8 times. Repeat from * around, ending as on 20th rnd.
Next 6 rnds: Repeat 21st rnd.
87th rnd: Ch 1, sc in loop just formed, (ch 7, sc in next loop) twice; * (ch 5, sc in next loop) 3 times; (ch 7, sc in next loop) 19 times. Repeat from * around, ending as on 20th rnd.
Next 11 rnds: Repeat 22nd rnd.
Next 6 rnds: Repeat 27th rnd.
105th rnd: Ch 1, sc in loop just formed, * 5 tr in next sc—shell made; sc in next loop, ch 9, sc in next loop. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 9. Join to first sc.
106th rnd: Sl st to center of next shell, sc in center st of same shell, * ch 3; in center ch of next loop make tr, ch 4, sl st in tr just made—picot made, 2 tr, picot, tr, picot, 2 tr, picot and tr; ch 3, sc in center st of next shell. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join to first sc. Break off and fasten. Block and press.
