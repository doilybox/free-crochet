Free Vintage Crochet - Stepping Stones Tablecloth Pattern

MATERIALS:
J. & P. COATS "BIG BALL" BEST SIX CORD MERCERIZED CROCHET, Size 30: 17 balls of No. 1 White, No. 61 Ecru
or
No. 42 Cream; or 25 balls of any color;
or
CLARK'S "BIG BALL" MERCERIZED CROCHET, Size 30: 13 balls of No. 1 White, No. 61 Ecru or No. 42 Cream; or 17 balls of any color.
Milwards Steel Crochet Hook No. 10.
Tablecloth measures 46 x 65 inches
GAUGE:
5 bls or sps = 1 inch;
5 rows = 1 inch. Starting at narrow edge, make a chain 49 inches long, having 15 ch to 1 inch.
1st row: (wrong side): Dc in 8th ch from hook (starting sp made); (ch 2, skip next 2 ch, dc in next ch—sp made) 210 times-211 sps made. Cut off any remaining chain.
NOTE: Hereafter at end of each row, ch 5, turn.
2nd row: Skip first dc, dc in next dc (starting sp over sp made); (ch 2, dc in next dc—sp over sp made) 4 times; 2 dc in next sp, dc in next dc (bl over sp made); * make 7 sps and 1 bl. Repeat from * across to within last 5 sps; make 4 sps, ch 2, skip first 2 ch of last sp, dc in next ch (sp over sp at end of row made).
3rd row: Make 4 sps, * make 1 bl; ch 9, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl and 5 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
4th row: Make 3 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 5, 3 sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl and 3 sps. Repeat from * across.
5th row: Make 2 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 5, sc in next loop, sc in next 3 sc, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 2 sps.
6th row: Make 1 sp, * make 1 bl, ch 7, sc in next loop, sc in next 5 sc, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 1 bl, 1 sp.
7th row: Make 1 sp, * ch 2, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc (sp over bl made); 3 dc in next loop (bl over loop made); ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 5 sc, ch 7, 3 dc in next loop (another bl over loop made); dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 2 sps.
8th row: Make 3 sps, * bl over loop, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 3 sc, ch 7, bl over loop, dc in next dc, make 3 sps. Repeat from * across.
9th row: Make 4 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 2, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 5 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
10th row: Work as for 2nd row to within last bl, make sp over bl, make 4 more sps.
11th row: Make 211 sps.
12th row: Make 10 sps, 1 bl, (18 sps, 1 bl) 10 times and 10 sps.
13th row: Make 9 sps and 1 bl; dc in next 3 dc (bl over bl made); make 1 bl, (16 sps, 3 bls) 10 times and 9 sps.
14th and 15th rows: Make 8 sps, 5 bls, (14 sps, 5 bls) 10 times and 8 sps.
16th row: Make 9 sps, 1 bl, ch 9, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, (16 sps, 1 bl, ch 9, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl) 10 times and 9 sps.
17th row: Make 5 sps, * make 2 bls, 1 sp and 1 bl, ch 5, 3 sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, 1 sp, 2 bls and 8 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 5 sps.
18th row: Make 4 sps, * make 4 bls, ch 5, sc in next loop, sc in next 3 sc, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 4 bls and 6 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
19th row: Make 3 sps, * make 4 bls, ch 7, sc in next loop, sc in next 5 sc, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 4 bls, 1 sp, 2 bls and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 3 sps.
20th row: Make 4 sps, * make 3 bls and 1 bl over loop, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 5 sc, ch 7, bl over loop, dc in next dc, make 3 bls, ch 3, skip next 2 dc, sc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next 5 dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, sc in next dc, ch 3, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
21st row: Make 5 sps, * make 2 bls, 1 sp and 1 bl, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 3 sc, ch 7, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 1 sp, 2 bls and 1 sp; ch 5, dc in next dc, (bar made); ch 3, sc in next sp, ch 3, dc in next dc (lacet made); make 1 bar and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 5 sps.
22nd row: Make 9 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 2, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 1 sp, ch 3, sc in next dc, ch 3, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 lacet, 1 bar and 1 lacet; ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip next 2 dc, sc in next dc, ch 3, dc in next dc, make 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
23rd row: Make 8 sps, * make 5 bls, (1 bar, 1 lacet) 3 times and 1 bar. Repeat from * across, ending with 8 sps.
24th row: Make 8 sps, * make 5 bls, (1 lacet, 1 bar) 3 times and 1 lacet. Repeat from * across, ending with 8 sps.
25th row: Make 9 sps, * make 3 bls, 1 sp, (1 bar, 1 lacet) 3 times; 1 bar and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
26th row: Make 10 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make (1 lacet, 1 bar) 3 times and 1 lacet; ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 10 sps.
27th row: Make 9 sps and 1 bl, * ch 9, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next sp, ch 2, dc in next dc, make (1 bar, 1 lacet) 3 times and 1 bar; ch 2, 3 dc in next sp, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with ch 9, 1 bl and 9 sps.
28th row: Make 8 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 5, 3 sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, (1 lacet, 1 bar) 3 times and 1 lacet. Repeat from * across, ending with 8 sps.
29th row: Make 7 sps and 1 bl, * ch 5, sc in next loop, sc in next 3 sc, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next ch-3, ch 2, dc in next dc, make (1 lacet, 1 bar) twice and 1 lacet; ch 2, skip next sc, 3 dc in next ch-3, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 7 sps.
30th row: Make 6 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 7, sc in next loop, sc m next 5 sc, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, (1 bar, 1 lacet) twice and 1 bar. Repeat from * across, ending with 6 sps.
31st row: Make 7 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 5 sc, ch 7, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 1 sp, (1 lacet, 1 bar) twice; 1 lacet and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 7 sps.
32nd row: Make 8 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 3 sc, ch 7, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 2 sps, (1 bar, 1 lacet) twice; 1 bar and 2 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 8 sps.
33rd row: Make 9 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 2, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 1 sp, ch 5, skip next dc, dc in next dc, make (1 lacet, 1 bar) twice and 1 lacet; ch 5, skip next dc, dc in next dc, make 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
34th row: Make 10 sps, * make 1 bl, 2 sps, (1 lacet, 1 bar) 3 times; 1 lacet and 2 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 10 sps.
35th row: Make 9 sps, * make 3 bls, 1 sp, (1 bar, 1 lacet) 3 times; 1 bar and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
Next 2 rows: Repeat 24th and 23rd rows.
38th row: Make 9 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 9, skip next 2 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, 1 sp, (1 lacet, 1 bar) 3 times; 1 lacet and 1 sp. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
39th row: Make 5 sps, 2 bls and 1 sp, * make 1 bl, ch 5, 3 sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 1 bl, ch 2, skip next sc, 3 dc in next ch-3, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next sp, ch 2, dc in next dc, make 1 bar, 1 lacet and 1 bar, ch 2, 3 dc in next sp, dc in next dc, 3 dc in next ch-3, ch 2, dc in next dc. Repeat from * across, ending with 5 sps.
40th row: Make 4 sps, * make 4 bls, ch 5, sc in next loop, sc in next 3 sc, sc in next loop, ch 5, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 4 bls, 1 lacet, 1 bar and 1 lacet. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
41st row: Make 3 sps, 4 bls, * ch 7, sc in next loop, sc in next 5 sc, sc in next loop, ch 7, skip next 3 dc, dc in next dc, make 3 bls, 3 dc in next ch-3, ch 2, dc in next dc, 5 dc over next bar, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip next sc, 3 dc in next ch-3, dc in next dc, make 3 bls. Repeat from * across, ending with 3 sps.
Repeat 20th through 41st row 12 times more (305 rows in all).
306th row: Make 4 sps, * make 3 bls and 1 bl over loop, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 5 sc, ch 7, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 3 bls and 6 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 4 sps.
307th row: Make 5 sps, * make 2 bls, 1 sp, and 1 bl, ch 7, skip next sc, sc in next 3 sc, ch 7, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 1 sp, 2 bls and 8 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 5 sps.
308th row: Make 9 sps, * make 1 bl, ch 2, make 1 bl, dc in next dc, make 16 sps. Repeat from * across, ending with 9 sps.
Next 2 rows: Repeat 14th row.
Following 3 rows: Repeat 13th, 12th and 11th rows.
Next 10 rows: Repeat 2nd through 1 1 th rows. There are 323 rows in all. Ch 5, do not turn.
Work Border along long edge as follows: 1st row: Working in ends of rows to keep continuity of bl or sp pattern, dc in same place as last dc, (ch 2, dc in base of next dc) 4 times-5 sps over last 5 rows; * 2 dc in next sp, dc in base of next dc, (ch 2, dc in base of next row) 7 times. Repeat from * across, ending with 5 sps. Ch 5, turn.
2nd through 9th row: Repeat 3rd through 10th row.
Last row: Make sps over sps and bls. Break off and fasten. Attach thread in corner sp and work along other long edge in same way.
EDGING: Attach thread in any corner sp, ch 3, ** dc in next sp, * ch 1, over last dc made make sc, h dc and 2 dc (shell made); skip next sp, dc in next dc, ch 1, shell over last dc made, dc in next sp. Repeat from * across to within next corner sp, ch 1, shell over last dc made, (dc in corner sp, ch 1, shell over last de made) twice. Repeat from ** around, ending with ch 1, shell over first ch-3 made. Break off and fasten. Starch lightly and block to measurements.
